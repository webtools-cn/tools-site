💡 Sleep Cycle Tips

  • Cycle Length: Each complete cycle is ~90 minutes (light → deep → REM)
  • Ideal Cycles: Adults need 4-6 cycles (6-9 hours)
  • Best Wake-up: Waking at cycle end (REM phase) leaves you most refreshed
  • Sweet Spot: 5 cycles (7.5h) + consistent schedule = best results
  • Napping: A 20-minute power nap (avoiding deep sleep) beats a 2-hour long nap
