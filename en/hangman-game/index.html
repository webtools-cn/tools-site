About Hangman Game

Free online Hangman word guessing game. Try to guess the hidden word one letter at a time. Each wrong guess adds a part to the hangman. Guess all letters before the drawing is complete to win!

How to play: Click letter keys on the keyboard to make guesses. Correct letters are revealed in the word. Wrong guesses cost one life. Choose from categories like Animals, Fruits, Countries, Sports, and Food.

Client-side only — no downloads, perfect for English practice and fun.
