💡 Usage Guide

The soil calculator helps you quickly estimate the amount of soil needed for raised beds, gardens, or planters — avoiding overbuying or running short.

Shape Options

  • Rectangle: For standard raised beds, planter boxes, or garden rows.
  • Circle: For round flower beds, large pots, or tree pits.
  • L-Shape: For corner beds or L-shaped gardens. Enter dimensions for both rectangular sections.

Depth Recommendations

  • Herbs: 6-8 in (15-20 cm)
  • Shrubs: 12-16 in (30-40 cm)
  • Vegetables: 8-12 in (20-30 cm)
  • Succulents: 4-6 in (10-15 cm)

❓ FAQ

Should I add extra to the result?

It's recommended to add 5-10% extra, as soil settles and compacts after filling. The calculator already rounds bag count up to the nearest whole number.

How do liters and cubic meters convert?

1 cubic meter = 1000 liters. This tool shows both units so you can use whichever your supplier offers.

Is my data uploaded to a server?

Absolutely not. All calculations run locally in your browser. No data leaves your device. You can use this tool offline.
