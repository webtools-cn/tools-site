📖 How to Use

Enter an energy value and select its unit. The tool will convert it to all supported units: Joules (J), Kilojoules (kJ), Calories (cal), Kilocalories (kcal), Kilowatt-hours (kWh), Electronvolts (eV), and BTU.

All calculations happen locally in your browser.
