❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Energy Converter free?

Yes, completely free. All conversions happen locally in your browser.

How many joules are in a calorie?

1 calorie (cal) = 4.184 joules (J). In nutrition, what's called a 'calorie' is actually a kilocalorie (kcal) = 4,184 J.

How do I convert kWh to Joules?

1 kWh = 3.6 × 10⁶ J = 3,600 kJ. kWh is the standard unit for electricity billing.

What is BTU and where is it used?

BTU (British Thermal Unit) is an imperial energy unit commonly used in air conditioning and heating. 1 BTU ≈ 1,055 J.

What is an electronvolt used for?

Electronvolts (eV) measure energy at the atomic scale, used in particle physics, quantum mechanics, and semiconductor physics. 1 eV ≈ 1.602 × 10⁻¹⁹ J.

Does this tool upload my data?

No. All energy conversions are done locally in your browser. No data is uploaded.
