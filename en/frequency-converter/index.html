Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is Frequency Converter free?

Yes, completely free. All conversions happen locally in your browser.

How many Hz is 1 MHz?

1 MHz = 1,000,000 Hz = 1,000 kHz. Megahertz is commonly used for radio broadcasts and processor clock speeds.

How to convert RPM to Hz?

1 Hz = 60 RPM. RPM (revolutions per minute) describes rotational speed. For example, 3,000 RPM = 50 Hz.

What is rad/s and how do I convert it?

rad/s (radians per second) is the SI unit of angular frequency. 1 Hz = 2π rad/s ≈ 6.283 rad/s. Used in physics and engineering for rotational systems.

Where is GHz used?

GHz (gigahertz) describes high-frequency signals like CPU clock speeds (3-5 GHz), WiFi frequencies (2.4/5 GHz), 5G communications, and satellite systems.

Does this tool upload my data?

No. All frequency conversions are done locally in your browser. No data is uploaded.
