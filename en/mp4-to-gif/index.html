📖 FAQ

What's the max duration?

We recommend 10 seconds or less. GIF size increases rapidly with frames. A 10s 15fps GIF is ~3-8MB. 30 second limit.

Why no sound in the GIF?

GIF format doesn't support audio. For sound, keep the video format. GIFs are ideal for silent looping clips.

How to make GIF smaller?

Lower frame rate (10-12fps is enough), reduce width to ≤480px, limit colors to 128, trim to essential clip.

What video formats are supported?

All browser-playable formats: MP4, WebM, OGG. Client-side processing — no upload.
