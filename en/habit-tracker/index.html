FAQ

How does habit tracking help?

Studies show it takes 21-66 days to form a new habit. This tool uses daily check-ins, streak tracking, and visual progress to keep you motivated and make behaviors automatic.

Will I lose my data?

Data is stored in your browser's localStorage. It persists across sessions but clearing cache or switching devices will erase it. Take screenshots to backup.

What is a streak?

A streak is the number of consecutive days you've completed a habit. It resets if you miss a day. Maintaining a streak is a powerful motivator for habit formation.

What habits can I track?

Any habit you want to build: reading, exercise, meditation, waking early, drinking water, writing, language learning, etc. Focus on 2-3 habits at a time.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe when using Habit Tracker?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server, so there's zero risk of data leaks. Your information stays completely under your control.

Is Habit Tracker free to use?

Yes, Habit Tracker is completely free to use. No registration or login required — all features are available immediately.

Does Habit Tracker upload my data to a server?

No. Habit Tracker uses client-side processing — everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, and the tool works even without an internet connection.

Can I use Habit Tracker on my phone?

Yes. Habit Tracker is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The interface adapts automatically to your screen size.

Are there any usage limits for Habit Tracker?

Habit Tracker has no usage limits — it's free and unlimited. Since processing is done client-side, speed depends on your device. For very large files, we recommend processing in batches for the best experience.

Do I need to install any software?

No. Habit Tracker is a web-based tool — just open it in your browser and start using it. No downloads, plugins, or extensions required.
