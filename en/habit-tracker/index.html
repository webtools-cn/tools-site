FAQ

How does habit tracking help?

Studies show it takes 21-66 days to form a new habit. This tool uses daily check-ins, streak tracking, and visual progress to keep you motivated and make behaviors automatic.

Will I lose my data?

Data is stored in your browser's localStorage. It persists across sessions but clearing cache or switching devices will erase it. Take screenshots to backup.

What is a streak?

A streak is the number of consecutive days you've completed a habit. It resets if you miss a day. Maintaining a streak is a powerful motivator for habit formation.

What habits can I track?

Any habit you want to build: reading, exercise, meditation, waking early, drinking water, writing, language learning, etc. Focus on 2-3 habits at a time.
