How to Use the Text Sentiment Analyzer

Converting with the Text Sentiment Analyzer takes just three steps:

  1. Enter the Value to Convert
    Type or paste the value you want to convert into the input area. The text sentiment analyzer supports various input formats — just enter your data and the tool will recognize it automatically.
  2. Select Source and Target Formats
    Choose the source format and the target format from the available options. The tool supports bidirectional conversion where applicable. Your settings are applied instantly.
  3. Get Your Conversion Result
    The converted result appears in real time. Click "Copy" to copy it to your clipboard, or "Download" to save it. All conversion is done locally on your device with no server upload.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the sentiment analysis?

The analyzer uses a comprehensive sentiment lexicon with thousands of positive and negative words. It provides a good approximation of text sentiment but may not capture nuanced emotions or sarcasm perfectly.

Does it support multiple languages?

Yes! It has built-in support for both English and Chinese sentiment words, and can analyze mixed-language text.

What do the scores mean?

The sentiment score ranges from -100 (very negative) to +100 (very positive). Scores near 0 indicate neutral text. The bar visualization shows the overall sentiment at a glance.

Is my text sent to a server?

No! All analysis runs locally in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.
