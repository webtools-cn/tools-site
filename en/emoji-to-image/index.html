❓ FAQ

How does Emoji to Image work?

Enter or paste an emoji, customize size (32-1024px), background color, and border radius, then click 'Export as PNG' to download. The live preview updates in real time.

Are all emojis supported?

All standard Unicode emojis are supported, including Unicode 15.0+. However, emoji rendering depends on your operating system and browser (Apple, Google Noto, Windows Fluent, etc.).

Can I use a transparent background?

Yes. Toggle 'Transparent Background' on to export PNG with alpha channel — perfect for icons and design overlays.

What size should I choose?

Social media: 128-256px. App icons: 512px. Print/design: 1024px for maximum clarity. The output PNG maintains sharp edges at any size.

Can I use the results commercially?

Emoji are Unicode standard characters, but emoji designs from specific platforms may have copyright restrictions. This tool simply renders your selected emoji — no watermarks are added.
