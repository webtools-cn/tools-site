📖 FAQ

Where can I use fancy text?

Most Unicode special characters work on Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, WhatsApp, Discord, Snapchat, and many other social platforms. Some fonts may not display correctly on certain platforms.

Will my account get banned for using fancy text?

This tool only uses legitimate Unicode characters. No banned or restricted content is involved. However, please don't use fancy text for spamming or violating platform rules.

Why can't I see some characters?

Not all fonts are available on every device. Some decorative Unicode characters require specific fonts that may not be installed on your system. This is normal and varies by device and operating system.
