About Circle Cropping

Circle cropping (also called circular crop or round crop) is a technique for cutting images into a circular shape. Common uses include:

• Profile pictures — Social media, forums, and professional profiles

• App icons — Mobile app and brand icons often use circular designs

• Product photos — E-commerce product images with circular crops

• Design assets — Circular images for UI design, presentations, and posters

All processing happens locally in your browser. Images are never uploaded to any server.

FAQ

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All image processing is done entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images never leave your device, making this safe for sensitive photos.

What image formats are supported?

All browser-supported formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, and SVG. Output is PNG format with your chosen background color.

Why is the background black/white?

The area outside the circle is filled with the background color you choose. By default it's set to #0f172a (dark). Change it to any color or match your website's background.
