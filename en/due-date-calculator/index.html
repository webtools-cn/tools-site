📖 How to Use

This Due Date Calculator helps expectant mothers estimate their due date, current pregnancy week, and trimester. Based on LMP or conception date. For reference only - always consult your healthcare provider.

How to Use

  1. Choose calculation method (LMP or conception date)
  2. Enter your LMP or conception date
  3. Due date, current week, and trimester are calculated automatically

About Due Dates

Due dates are calculated using Naegele's Rule: LMP + 280 days (40 weeks). Only about 4% of babies are born on their exact due date. Most arrive within 1-2 weeks before or after.
