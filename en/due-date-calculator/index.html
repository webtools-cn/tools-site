📖 How to Use

This Due Date Calculator helps expectant mothers estimate their due date, current pregnancy week, and trimester. Based on LMP or conception date. For reference only - always consult your healthcare provider.

How to Use

  1. Choose calculation method (LMP or conception date)
  2. Enter your LMP or conception date
  3. Due date, current week, and trimester are calculated automatically

About Due Dates

Due dates are calculated using Naegele's Rule: LMP + 280 days (40 weeks). Only about 4% of babies are born on their exact due date. Most arrive within 1-2 weeks before or after.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is the Due Date Calculator free?

Yes, it's completely free. No registration or login required.

How is due date calculated?

The most common method is Naegele's Rule: LMP + 280 days (40 weeks). Conception date + 266 days is also used.

Does it upload my data to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device and stays completely private.

How accurate is the due date?

Due date is an estimate. Only about 4% of babies are born on their exact due date. Most arrive within 1-2 weeks before or after. Always consult your healthcare provider.

What do pregnancy weeks mean?

Pregnancy weeks are counted from the first day of your last menstrual period. A full pregnancy is about 40 weeks, divided into three trimesters.
