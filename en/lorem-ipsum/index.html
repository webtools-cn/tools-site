About Lorem Ipsum

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is the standard placeholder text used in the printing and typesetting industry. It originates from "De finibus bonorum et malorum" (Extremes of Good and Evil) by the Roman philosopher Cicero, written in 45 BC, specifically sections 1.10.32-33 of the first book. It has been used as placeholder text since the 1500s, with a history of over 500 years.

Why Use Lorem Ipsum?

There are several important reasons to use Lorem Ipsum instead of arbitrary made-up text: it looks like normal text but has no meaningful content, so it doesn't distract readers from the layout design. Latin word length distribution is similar to English, providing a more realistic simulation of typesetting. It's the international standard that designers and developers instantly recognize as placeholder text.

The Classic Opening

Standard Lorem Ipsum always begins with Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. This is the opening of the original text and has become the signature feature of placeholder text. This tool defaults to starting with this phrase, but you can uncheck it for completely random text.

Tutorial

This Lorem Ipsum generator is simple to use with flexible options:

Choose Generation Type: Select paragraphs, sentences, or words from the "Type" dropdown. Paragraph mode is ideal for filling large layout spaces, sentence mode for precise text control, and word mode for short text or title placeholders.

Set Count: Enter the number of paragraphs (1-100), sentences (1-1000), or words (1-10000) to generate in the "Count" field. The tool generates the corresponding amount of text based on your selected type.

Options: Check "Start with Lorem ipsum" to begin with the classic Lorem Ipsum opening. Check "Use <p> tags" to wrap each paragraph in HTML paragraph tags, convenient for pasting directly into HTML code.

Copy & Download: After generation, click "Copy Result" to copy to clipboard, or "Download TXT" to save the text as a .txt file.

Use Cases

Web Design Prototypes: Fill text areas with Lorem Ipsum when creating web design mockups, helping clients focus on layout and visual effects rather than text content. This is the most common use case for designers.

Print Typesetting Tests: Use Lorem Ipsum during typesetting of books, magazines, and posters to test font effects, line spacing, letter spacing, and page layout before filling in final content.

Development Debugging: Frontend developers use Lorem Ipsum to fill text content in list, card, and article components, testing how components perform with varying text lengths.

Presentation Documents: Fill sample content with Lorem Ipsum when creating product demos or presentations, avoiding privacy issues from using real data.

Extended Knowledge

History of Lorem Ipsum: In the 1980s, with the rise of desktop publishing software, Lorem Ipsum was widely integrated into design tools. Early typesetting software like Aldus PageMaker introduced templates containing Lorem Ipsum paragraphs, making it an industry standard. Today, virtually all design software and CMS systems include built-in Lorem Ipsum generation.

Alternative Placeholder Text: Besides Lorem Ipsum, there are Hipster Ipsum (using modern slang), Cupcake Ipsum (dessert-themed), Zombie Ipsum (zombie-themed), and other fun placeholder texts. However, Lorem Ipsum remains the most popular choice due to its neutral, professional, and historically established nature.

Non-Latin Placeholder Text: For CJK (Chinese, Japanese, Korean) typography, classical texts or random characters are sometimes used as placeholders. However, since CJK characters are fixed-width, the typesetting test results differ significantly from Latin text, so Lorem Ipsum is still recommended for international projects.
