❓ FAQ

How do I merge PDF files?

1) Upload multiple PDFs (click or drag & drop); 2) Drag to reorder files; 3) Click 'Merge PDFs', download the combined file. All processing is done locally in your browser — files never leave your device.

Will merging change the original formatting?

No! This tool uses the pdf-lib library and preserves original formatting, fonts, images, and page sizes. All pages from each PDF are merged in order.

What are the file size and page limits?

Individual files up to 50MB recommended. Total pages under 500 for best performance. If you encounter memory issues, try merging in batches.

Is the merged file secure?

Extremely secure! All processing is local — your PDFs never leave your device. We cannot see or access any of your file contents.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does PDF Merger upload my data to a server?

No. PDF Merger uses client-side processing — everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, and the tool works even without an internet connection.

Is my data safe when using PDF Merger?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server, so there's zero risk of data leaks. Your information stays completely under your control.

Are there any usage limits for PDF Merger?

PDF Merger has no usage limits — it's free and unlimited. Since processing is done client-side, speed depends on your device. For very large files, we recommend processing in batches for the best experience.

Do I need to install any software?

No. PDF Merger is a web-based tool — just open it in your browser and start using it. No downloads, plugins, or extensions required.

Can I use PDF Merger on my phone?

Yes. PDF Merger is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The interface adapts automatically to your screen size.

Will the PDF quality decrease after processing?

We preserve original quality whenever possible. For compression, you can choose the compression level to balance file size and quality.
