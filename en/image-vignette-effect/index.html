Frequently Asked Questions

What is a vignette effect?

A vignette darkens the edges of an image, drawing attention to the center. It's inspired by photographic lens effects where the corners naturally receive less light, creating a subtle or dramatic frame around the subject.

Can I customize the vignette color?

Yes! While black is the most common choice for vignettes, you can select any color for the darkened edges. This allows creative effects like white vignettes or colored corner shadows.

How do Strength and Radius work?

Strength controls how dark the edges become (0% = no effect, 100% = fully black). Radius controls how far from the center the vignette starts - lower values create a larger dark area, higher values keep the center brighter longer.
