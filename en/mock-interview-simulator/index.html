Mock Interview Simulator - Essential Interview Prep Tool

The Mock Interview Simulator helps job seekers systematically prepare for interviews. Covering technical, behavioral, and system design interviews with random questions, reference answers, and scoring criteria.

Interview Types

  • Technical Interview - Algorithms, data structures, coding, debugging
  • Behavioral Interview - STAR method, teamwork, leadership, conflict resolution
  • System Design - Distributed systems, database design, caching, scalability

FAQ

What types of interview questions are available?

Three main types: Technical, Behavioral, and System Design interviews. Each type has multiple difficulty levels.

How to use the mock interview tool?

Select interview type and difficulty, click start. Think through your answer, then check the reference answer and scoring criteria. Use the timer for realistic pressure.

How good are the reference answers?

Reference answers are based on industry best practices, including complete solution approaches, key points, and common pitfalls.

Who is this tool for?

Anyone preparing for interviews: new graduates, career changers, senior engineers, and management candidates.
