What is a Gratitude Journal?

A gratitude journal is a positive psychology practice that cultivates optimism and well-being by recording things you are grateful for each day. Studies show consistent gratitude practice significantly improves mental health, reducing anxiety and depression symptoms. Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of a gratitude journal?

Research shows that daily gratitude journaling significantly boosts happiness and life satisfaction. It shifts focus from negative to positive, reduces stress, improves sleep quality, and strengthens relationships. Long-term gratitude practice can rewire neural pathways, making it easier to feel joy and contentment.

How to write a gratitude journal?

Write 3 things you're grateful for each day, big or small. They can be simple like 'had a great cup of coffee,' 'a colleague helped me,' or 'the weather was beautiful today.' The key is to be specific and genuine. Just 3-5 minutes per day is enough.

Is my gratitude journal data secure?

All data is stored in your browser's local storage and never uploaded to any server. It works offline. However, clearing browser cache or using private mode may cause data loss. We recommend regular export backups.
