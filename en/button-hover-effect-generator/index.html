FAQ

What types of button hover effects are available?

Common button hover effects include: 1) Gradient fill/color swap 2) Border slide animation 3) Scale zoom 4) Glow/radiant effect 5) Neon border 6) Icon slide or rotate 7) Underline expand 8) Background fill 9) 3D press effect 10) Ripple spread. This tool supports multiple presets.

How to choose the right button hover effect?

Choose based on brand style and application: corporate sites - clean fill or border effects; creative sites - neon or glow effects; CTA buttons - scale or fill effects for higher click rates; gaming sites - neon or 3D effects for engaging interactions.

Do these hover effects work on mobile devices?

The button base styles work perfectly on mobile. Hover effects on touch devices trigger on tap (first tap shows hover state, second tap triggers the button action). Focus styles are recommended for keyboard navigation accessibility.
