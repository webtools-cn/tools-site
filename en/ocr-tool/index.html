Frequently Asked Questions

What does this OCR tool do?
This is an online OCR tool powered by Tesseract.js. It extracts text from images in JPG, PNG, or WebP format. Supports English and Chinese. All processing runs locally in your browser. Language packs are downloaded automatically on first use.
How accurate is the OCR?
Typically 90%+ for clear images with high contrast and standard fonts. Handwriting and blurry images yield lower accuracy. For best results, use images with at least 150 DPI.
Are images uploaded to a server?
No. All processing runs locally via Tesseract.js WebAssembly. Images and text never leave your device. Data is cleared when you close the page.
Which languages are supported?
English (eng) and Simplified Chinese (chi_sim) by default. Language packs download automatically on first use and are cached for future sessions.
