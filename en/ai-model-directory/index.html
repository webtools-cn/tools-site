What Can the AI Model Directory Do?

The AI Model Directory is a free online tool that aggregates and compares specs, pricing, and capabilities of major AI models including GPT-4o, Claude 4, Gemini 2.5, Llama 4, DeepSeek V3, and Mistral Large. Filter by vendor and type, sort by context window, pricing, and more to find the best model for your needs.

Core Features

  • Model Overview: 10+ major AI models at a glance
  • Price Comparison: Clear input/output token pricing
  • Filter & Sort: By vendor, open/closed source, price
  • Context Comparison: 128K to 10M context windows
  • Regular Updates: Model data kept current

Use Cases

API Selection

When building AI apps, compare pricing and capabilities to find the best fit for your budget and requirements.

Cost Optimization

Reduce API costs by comparing models and finding cheaper alternatives, like GPT-4o-mini instead of GPT-4o.

Tech Research

Stay updated on the latest AI models, compare open vs closed source capabilities, and make data-driven technology decisions.

Background

The 2026 AI model landscape: OpenAI's GPT series leads in general capability, Anthropic's Claude excels in safety and long-form, Google's Gemini features multimodal and ultra-long context, Meta's Llama is the open-source benchmark, and DeepSeek has rapidly emerged with ultra-low pricing and open-source strategy. When choosing a model, consider: task type (code/text/multimodal), context needs, budget, latency requirements, and data privacy (local deployment needs).
