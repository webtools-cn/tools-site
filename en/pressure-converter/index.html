❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pressure Converter free?

Yes, completely free. No registration needed. All conversions happen locally in your browser.

How many PSI is 1 bar?

1 bar equals approximately 14.5038 PSI. Bar is commonly used in EU countries while PSI is used in the US and UK.

How do I convert MPa to PSI?

1 MPa = 145.0377 PSI. Megapascal is commonly used in hydraulic systems and material strength engineering.

What is mmHg and where is it used?

mmHg (millimeters of mercury) is used for blood pressure measurements (e.g., 120/80 mmHg), vacuum systems, and meteorology. 1 mmHg ≈ 133.322 Pa.

How does atmospheric pressure convert to Pascals?

1 standard atmosphere (atm) = 101,325 Pascals (Pa) = 1.01325 bar = 14.696 PSI. This is the average atmospheric pressure at sea level.

Does this tool upload my data?

No. All pressure conversions are done locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.
