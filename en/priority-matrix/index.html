❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Eisenhower Matrix?

The Eisenhower Matrix is a time management method named after President Dwight Eisenhower. It categorizes tasks by urgency and importance into four quadrants: Urgent & Important, Important Not Urgent, Urgent Not Important, Neither.

How to use the matrix for productivity?

List all tasks and sort them by urgency and importance. Focus on urgent & important first, invest time in important but not urgent tasks to prevent crises, minimize urgent but not important tasks.

Where is my data saved?

All task data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Data persists when you close and reopen the page.

Can I export my tasks?

Currently you can screenshot the matrix for backup. Data is stored locally in your browser.
