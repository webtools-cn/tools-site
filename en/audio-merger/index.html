How to Merge Audio Files

Merging audio is simple:

  1. Upload Audio Files
    Click or drag to upload multiple audio files. Supports MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A.
  2. Manage File List
    View uploaded files. Remove unwanted ones. Order determines merge sequence.
  3. Merge & Download
    Click "Start Merge". Download the combined WAV file when done.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What does the audio merger do?
Combines multiple audio files into a single file in sequence. Perfect for merging songs, voice clips, or audio segments.
What formats are supported?
MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A and other common formats. Decoded via Web Audio API.
Can I reorder the files?
Yes. Remove and re-upload files in desired order. Merging follows the list order.
Is my audio uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Files never leave your device.
What format is the output?
Exported as WAV format at the original sample rate.
Can I merge files of different formats?
Yes. Different formats can be mixed. The tool decodes all files to a unified format before merging.
