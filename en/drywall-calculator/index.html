💡 How to Use

  • Enter room length, width, and wall height
  • Choose whether to include the ceiling
  • Enter total door/window area to deduct
  • Select the drywall sheet size
  • Set a waste factor (typically 10%)
  • Click "Calculate" for results

📏 Common Sheet Sizes

  • 4' × 8' — Most common standard
  • 4' × 10' — For higher ceilings
  • 4' × 12' — Minimizes seams on tall walls
  • 4' × 16' — For large commercial spaces

📋 Formula

  • Wall area = perimeter × wall height
  • Ceiling area = length × width (optional)
  • Net area = total area − openings
  • With waste = net area × (1 + waste%)
  • Sheets = ⌈with waste ÷ sheet area⌉
