📖 FAQ

What is running pace?

Pace is the time to run one km or mile, expressed as min:sec per unit (e.g., 5:30/km). It's one of the most important running metrics.

How to calculate pace?

Pace = Total Time ÷ Distance. Enter Distance+Time to calculate pace, or Distance+Pace to calculate finish time.

How to convert between distances?

Marathon pace is typically 30-60 sec/km slower than 5K pace. This tool supports km/mile dual units with auto conversion.

What is negative split?

Running the second half faster than the first. An advanced strategy to avoid hitting the wall in long races.

What units are supported?

Kilometers (km) and miles (mi). 1 mi ≈ 1.609 km. Pace conversion: min/mile = min/km × 1.609.
