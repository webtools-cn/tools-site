FAQ

Do I need to install anything?

不需要安装任何软件。这是一个纯浏览器端的PDF阅读器，使用PDF.js库在浏览器中渲染PDF文件。只需打开网，选择或拖入PDF文件即可立即查看，支持所有现代浏览器（Chrome、Firefox、Edge、Safari）。

Will my PDF be uploaded to a server?

不会。所有PDF文件的处理完全在您的浏览器本地完成。文件不会被上传到任何服务器，我们也不会收集、存储或访问您的文件内容。这是一个注重隐私的工具，您可以放心使用。

What PDF features are supported?

当前版本支持PDF面渲染、缩放（50%-200%）、面导航、文本选择和复制。对于更高级的功能（如表单填写、注释添加），建议使用桌面PDF阅读器如Adobe Acrobat或Foxit Reader。

What size PDFs are supported?

理论上没有硬性大小限制，但受浏览器内存限制。对于超过100MB的PDF文件，加载可能会变慢。建议将大型PDF分割为较小的文件以获得更流畅的阅读体验。

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any usage limits for PDF Reader?

PDF Reader has no usage limits — it's free and unlimited. Since processing is done client-side, speed depends on your device. For very large files, we recommend processing in batches for the best experience.

Is PDF Reader free to use?

Yes, PDF Reader is completely free to use. No registration or login required — all features are available immediately.

Can I use PDF Reader on my phone?

Yes. PDF Reader is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The interface adapts automatically to your screen size.

Does PDF Reader upload my data to a server?

No. PDF Reader uses client-side processing — everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, and the tool works even without an internet connection.

Will the PDF quality decrease after processing?

We preserve original quality whenever possible. For compression, you can choose the compression level to balance file size and quality.
