📖 How to Use Screen Recorder

  1. Configure options — Toggle system audio and microphone recording as needed
  2. Click "Start Recording" — Your browser will prompt you to select a screen, window, or tab to record
  3. Recording in progress — The timer shows your recording length. You can minimize the browser while recording continues
  4. Stop and download — Click "Stop Recording" when finished — the video file downloads automatically

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the screen recorder tool free?

Yes, it's completely free to use with no registration or software installation required. No usage or time limits whatsoever.

Where are the recordings saved?

Recordings are automatically downloaded to your computer after recording stops. All processing is done locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

Why does the browser ask me to select what to share?

This is a built-in browser security feature. When a webpage requests screen recording access, the browser requires you to explicitly choose what to share (entire screen, application window, or browser tab) to prevent unauthorized recording.

What format is the recorded video?

The video is saved as WebM format with VP8/VP9 video codec and Opus audio codec. It can be played directly in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and most modern browsers.

How long can I record for?

There is no recording time limit. However, longer recordings result in larger files — about 1 hour of recording uses approximately 1-2GB of memory. Make sure you have enough free memory for longer recordings.
