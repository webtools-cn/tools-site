How to Use

  1. Enter your trip distance (km or miles)
  2. Choose fuel economy unit (L/100km or MPG) and enter the value
  3. Enter the fuel price (multiple currencies supported)
  4. Click Calculate to get total fuel cost, fuel needed, and cost per km/mile

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I reduce fuel costs?

Drive at steady speeds, avoid rapid acceleration and braking, maintain your vehicle regularly, keep tires properly inflated, and reduce excess weight.

Q: What's the difference between L/100km and MPG?

L/100km measures liters per 100 kilometers (lower is better). MPG measures miles per US gallon (higher is better). They are inversely related.

Q: Does the result include tolls?

No. This tool only calculates fuel costs. Highway tolls, parking fees, and other expenses need to be estimated separately.

Q: What currencies are supported?

Supports USD ($), EUR (€), GBP (£), CNY (¥), JPY (¥), and more. Select the currency symbol that matches your local fuel price.

Q: Why does actual cost differ from the estimate?

Actual fuel consumption varies with traffic conditions, driving habits, vehicle load, AC usage, and weather. This is an estimate for budgeting purposes.
