What is the Online Weekly Planner?

The Online Weekly Planner is a free visual schedule management tool that helps you plan tasks and appointments on a weekly basis. All data is saved locally in your browser — no registration, no installation, just open and start planning.

Features

  • Visual week view: See all 7 days at a glance with your task layout
  • Priority levels: High/Medium/Low priority with color-coded indicators
  • Auto-save: Data is automatically saved in your browser and restored on next visit
  • Completion tracking: Check off completed tasks and track your completion rate
  • Export & share: Export as JSON file or copy as text to clipboard

Tips for Effective Weekly Planning

Spend 15 minutes at the start of each week to plan. Break large tasks into smaller steps. Prioritize — do important things first. Leave buffer time for unexpected tasks. Review each day and adjust the next day's plan.

Is my weekly plan data saved?
Yes. All data is automatically saved in your browser's localStorage and restored when you reopen the page. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
How do I add tasks?
Click the input box under any day column, type your task, and press Enter. You can set priority (High/Medium/Low) before adding.
How do I mark tasks as complete?
Click the checkbox next to the task. Completed tasks show a strikethrough. Click again to undo.
Can I export my weekly plan?
Yes. Click 'Export' to save as JSON, or 'Copy' to copy the plan text to clipboard.
Is my data uploaded to a server?
No. All data stays in your browser. Your schedule is completely private.
