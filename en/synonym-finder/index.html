FAQ

What is the synonym finder?

It is an online tool that looks up synonyms and antonyms for an English word from a built-in local thesaurus, helping you enrich vocabulary and polish your writing.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All lookups happen entirely in your browser. The thesaurus is embedded in the page and no data is sent to any server.

Does it support other languages?

Currently the tool supports English words because English synonym dictionaries are well-structured and easy to embed. Support for other languages may come in future updates.

How many words does the thesaurus contain?

The built-in thesaurus includes hundreds of common English words with their synonyms and antonyms, covering everyday writing, academic, and business vocabulary.
