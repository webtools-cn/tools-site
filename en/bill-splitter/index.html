What is a Bill Splitter?

A bill splitter helps you quickly divide expenses for restaurants, travel, shared housing, and more. Enter the total amount, number of people, tip percentage, and tax rate to get each person's share. Supports custom ratios for fair splitting based on consumption, income, or custom weights. All calculations are local in your browser.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to use the Bill Splitter?

It's simple: enter the total bill amount, number of people, tip percentage (typically 15%-20%), and tax rate, then click calculate. You'll get each person's share instantly. For uneven splits (if someone ordered more expensive items), you can enter custom split ratios and the tool adjusts automatically.

How much tip should I leave?

In the US and Canada, restaurant tips typically range from 15%-25%. In Europe, service charges are often included, but an extra 5%-10% is appreciated. In China, tipping isn't expected at regular restaurants, though high-end places may add a 10%-15% service charge.

How to split a bill fairly?

There are several fair ways to split bills: equal split (everyone pays the same) works when consumption is similar; proportional split allocates based on each person's order amount or income; going Dutch (each pays their own items) is fairest but most tedious. This tool supports all methods for any scenario.
