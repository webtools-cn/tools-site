❓ Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is Schema structured data?
Schema.org provides a vocabulary for structured data markup on web pages. JSON-LD Schema tells search engines and AI systems what your content means. For example, FAQPage Schema enables rich results with Q&A boxes in search, and Article Schema enhances article display.
