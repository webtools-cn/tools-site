📖 How to Use

Enter an angle value and select its unit. The tool converts it to all supported units: degrees (°), radians (rad), gradians (grad), arcminutes ('), arcseconds (\"). Also supports DMS (degrees/minutes/seconds) to decimal format conversion.

All calculations happen locally in your browser.
