Frequently Asked Questions

How does the dominant color extraction work?

The tool uses K-means clustering algorithm to analyze all pixels in the image and group them into color clusters. The center of each cluster represents a dominant color.

How many colors can I extract?

You can extract 1 to 10 dominant colors. The default is 5 colors, which provides a good balance between accuracy and usability.

Can I copy the color palette?

Yes! Click the "Copy All" button to copy all extracted colors (HEX values) to your clipboard, ready to paste into your design tools.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No! All image processing is done locally in your browser using Canvas API. Your image never leaves your device.
