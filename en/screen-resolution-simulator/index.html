How to Use the Screen Resolution Simulator

Process your images with the Screen Resolution Simulator in three steps:

  1. Upload Your Image
    Click the upload area or drag and drop an image file. The tool supports common formats including PNG, JPG, GIF, SVG, and WebP. Your image stays on your device at all times.
  2. Configure Image Settings
    Adjust image processing options such as dimensions, quality, filters, or effects. Preview changes in real time before applying them.
  3. Download the Processed Image
    Once you are satisfied with the result, click "Download" to save the processed image. The tool never uploads your images to any server.

FAQ

What is a screen resolution simulator?

It tests how websites display at different device resolutions, helping developers debug responsive design.

What devices are supported?

iPhone, iPad, Android phones, Android tablets, MacBook, desktop monitors, and custom resolutions.

Is the simulator accurate?

It uses iframe and CSS scaling to simulate resolutions, accurately previewing layout changes but not device-specific rendering.
