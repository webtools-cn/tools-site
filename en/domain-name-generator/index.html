💡 Domain Naming Tips

1. Keep it Short

Short domains are easier to remember and type. Aim for under 15 characters.

2. Avoid Numbers and Hyphens

Numbers and hyphens can cause confusion (is it 2 or 'to'?). Pure letter combinations work best.

3. Reflect Your Business

Your domain should give visitors an idea of what your website is about.

4. Choose the Right TLD

.com is king, .io/.dev for tech, .org for non-profits, .app for mobile apps.

5. Get Creative

If your first choice is taken, try prefixes like get/my/best, or combine two relevant words.

❓ FAQ

Can I use the generated domains directly?

The domains generated are suggestions only. Always verify availability at a domain registrar like GoDaddy or Namecheap before purchasing.

How to get the best suggestions?

Try multiple keywords. Combinations often yield better results than single words. Select multiple TLDs for more options.

What if .com is taken?

Try: adding prefixes (get/my/best), alternative TLDs, combining two words, abbreviations, or synonyms.
