❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert screenshots to PDF?

Upload or drag your screenshots (PNG/JPG/WebP), choose page size (A4/Letter/Original), and click "Generate & Download PDF". Multiple images are merged into one PDF with one image per page.

What image formats are supported?

PNG, JPG/JPEG, WebP, BMP, and GIF (first frame). Up to 50 images can be converted and merged into one PDF at once.

Will image quality degrade in the PDF?

This tool uses Canvas high-quality rendering. Default A4 size (595×842pt, ~72DPI) auto-scales images while preserving aspect ratio. Select "Original Size" mode to retain original pixel dimensions.

Can I search for text in the generated PDF?

No. This tool embeds images as image objects in the PDF without OCR text recognition. For searchable PDFs, use a dedicated OCR tool.
