FAQ

Why track expenses?

Expense tracking is the foundation of personal finance. By recording every transaction, you'll know where your money goes, find unnecessary spending, control budgets, and increase savings.

Is my data safe?

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Data persists across sessions but clearing cache will erase it.

Can I export data?

Currently data is stored in browser localStorage. Take screenshots of monthly summaries for backup. You can also export localStorage data via browser dev tools.

Can I customize categories?

Preset categories include Food, Transport, Shopping, Entertainment, Housing, and Other. Choose the closest match for accurate monthly statistics.
