📖 How to Use

Free online Word Cloud Generator. Paste text to automatically generate a beautiful word cloud image. High-frequency words appear larger.

Customize the maximum number of words and download the result as PNG. Perfect for text analysis, presentations, and content summarization.

🎯 Use Cases

  • Text Analysis: Quickly identify key topics in articles or reviews
  • Presentations: Visualize keywords in slides
  • Resume Skills: Showcase your tech stack as a word cloud
  • Content Summary: Extract core themes at a glance

❓ FAQ

How is the word cloud generated?

The system analyzes word frequency in your text and arranges them on canvas with varying sizes—more frequent words appear larger. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Does it support Chinese?

Yes, it supports both Chinese and English text. Chinese characters are analyzed as individual tokens, English by words.

Can I customize colors?

Colors are automatically assigned from a preset palette for visual variety. Each word gets a random color from the selection.
