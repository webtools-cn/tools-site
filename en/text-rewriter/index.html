❓ FAQ

How is this different from AI rewriting?

This tool uses rule-based smart rewriting: synonym replacement, sentence restructuring, and active/passive voice conversion. No external AI API calls — all processing happens locally in your browser. It's completely free and privacy-safe. AI rewriting usually requires internet-connected models like GPT, which may have privacy risks and costs.

Will rewritten text pass plagiarism checks?

This tool reduces text similarity through synonym replacement and sentence restructuring. Results depend on original text complexity — simple sentences have limited changes, complex paragraphs yield better outcomes. We recommend reviewing and polishing the output yourself. This tool assists rewriting but cannot guarantee 100% plagiarism-free results.

Is my text uploaded to any server?

No. This tool is fully client-side — all text processing happens in your browser. We don't collect, upload, or store any of your content. You can safely process sensitive or private text with complete privacy.

What rewriting modes are available?

Three modes: Standard for everyday rewriting, Deep for more significant changes, and Light for removing redundancy and keeping things concise. Try Standard first, then switch modes based on results.
