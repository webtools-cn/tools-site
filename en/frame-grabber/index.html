📖 How to Use

Extracting Frames

Upload a video, use the timeline or frame-by-frame buttons to navigate to the desired position, then click "Capture Frame". You can also batch capture frames at set intervals.

Features

  • Frame-by-frame forward/backward navigation
  • Timeline scrubbing for quick seeking
  • Batch capture at custom intervals
  • PNG lossless image output
  • Click thumbnails to download

📖 FAQ

What video formats are supported?

All video formats playable by your browser are supported, including MP4, WebM, and OGG. Most modern browsers support MP4 (H.264) and WebM (VP8/VP9).

Is my video uploaded to a server?

No. All video processing happens entirely in your browser. Your video file is never uploaded to any server. Captured frames are generated locally.

What format are the captured images?

Captured images are in PNG format at the original video resolution. PNG uses lossless compression to ensure image quality.

Can I batch capture multiple frames?

Yes. You can set a time interval and batch capture multiple frames from the video with one click. You can also use the frame-by-frame buttons to precisely browse each frame.

How accurate is frame capture?

Accuracy depends on the video's keyframe distribution. For most MP4 videos, frame-level accuracy is achievable. Use the frame forward/backward buttons to precisely select the target frame.
