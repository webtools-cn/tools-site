❓ FAQ

What's the difference between flip and rotate?

Flip creates a mirror reflection along an axis. Horizontal flip swaps left/right, vertical swaps top/bottom. Rotation spins the image around its center. They produce different results.

Does flipping affect image quality?

No. Flipping only rearranges pixel positions — no compression or resampling. Quality stays 100% identical.

What image formats are supported?

All browser-compatible formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, GIF, SVG. Output can be PNG (recommended) or JPEG.

Can I flip multiple images at once?

Currently one image per session. Batch processing is planned for a future update.
