📖 How to Use

Paste two texts in the left and right panels, click 'Compare' to see the differences highlighted.

Green = additions, Red = deletions, Yellow = changes.

Supports case-insensitive and whitespace-ignoring options. All processing is done locally in your browser.

⚡ How It Works

This tool uses pure front-end technology. All data processing is done locally in your browser. No server support needed — your data never leaves your device, ensuring privacy and security.
