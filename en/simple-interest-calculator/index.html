❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Simple Interest Calculator free to use?

Yes, the Simple Interest Calculator is completely free. No registration or login required.

What is the difference between simple interest and compound interest?

Simple interest is calculated only on the principal amount, so each period's interest is the same. Compound interest adds each period's interest to the principal, so the next period earns interest on a larger base. This calculator handles simple interest only.

Does this calculator upload my data?

No. All calculations are done in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes. The calculator is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

What is the simple interest formula?

Simple Interest = Principal × Annual Rate × Time (years). Total = Principal + Interest. For example: $10,000 at 5% for 3 years = $10,000 × 0.05 × 3 = $1,500 interest, total $11,500.

What time periods are supported?

The calculator supports annual, monthly, and daily periods. Monthly rate = annual rate ÷ 12. Daily rate = annual rate ÷ 365.
