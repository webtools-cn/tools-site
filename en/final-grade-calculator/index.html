How to Use

  1. Enter your current grade (0-100%)
  2. Enter the final exam weight (e.g. 40 for 40%)
  3. Enter your target grade (what you want to end up with)
  4. Click Calculate to see what you need on the final
  5. Add multiple courses to plan your study strategy

How Final Grade Calculation Works

Formula: Final Score Needed = (Target Grade - Current Grade × (1 - Exam Weight)) ÷ Exam Weight

Example: Current 85%, Final worth 40%, Target 90%:

(90 - 85 × 0.6) ÷ 0.4 = (90 - 51) ÷ 0.4 = 97.5%

If the result exceeds 100%, even a perfect final exam won't reach your target.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What education levels is this for?

All levels: high school, college, and graduate school. Works for any course using weighted grade calculation.

Q: What if the result is over 100%?

It means that even a perfect score on the final won't reach your target. You may need to adjust your goal or improve your current grade through extra credit.

Q: What if the result is negative?

A negative result means you've already achieved your target grade! You could technically skip the final and still get your goal.

Q: Does this support letter grades?

The calculator uses percentage (0-100). Convert your letter grades to percentages first (A=90, B=80, C=70, etc.) based on your school's scale.

Q: Can I calculate multiple classes?

Yes! Click "Add Course" to add as many courses as you need. This helps you decide which finals to prioritize.

Q: Is my data private?

Absolutely. All calculations happen locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.
