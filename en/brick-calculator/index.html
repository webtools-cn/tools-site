About Brick Calculator

Free online brick calculator. Estimate how many bricks you need for your wall project. Enter wall dimensions (length, height, thickness) and brick size. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I calculate how many bricks I need?

Enter your wall dimensions (length, height, wall thickness), select the brick type and mortar thickness. The tool estimates the number of bricks and mortar volume needed.

What brick types are supported?

Standard brick sizes including US (7.5×3.5in), UK (215×102.5mm), modular, queen, king, and custom brick sizes.

Is this tool free?

Yes, this tool is 100% free to use online with no registration or payment required. No hidden charges.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All processing is done locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server or stored anywhere.

What browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are fully supported. The tool works on both desktop and mobile devices.

Are there usage limits?

No, you can use this tool as many times as you need. There are no daily limits or usage caps.

Does it work offline?

After the page loads, the tool works entirely in your browser. No internet connection is needed for processing.
