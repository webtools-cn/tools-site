💡 About This Tool

This text summarizer uses extractive summarization — it scores sentences based on word frequency, sentence position, and keywords, then selects the most important ones. It works completely in your browser: no data is ever sent to a server.

Perfect for quickly digesting long articles, research papers, news stories, or any lengthy text. Simply paste your content, adjust the summary length, and get a concise summary in seconds.

❓ FAQ

How does the summarizer work?

It uses extractive summarization: sentence scoring based on word frequency, sentence position, and keyword presence. The top-scoring sentences are selected to form a coherent summary.

Is my text sent to any server?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.

What's the maximum text length?

There's no hard limit, but for best performance keep text under 50,000 characters. Very long texts may impact browser responsiveness.
