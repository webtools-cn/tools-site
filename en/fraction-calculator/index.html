FAQ

How to use the fraction calculator?

Enter the numerators and denominators of two fractions, choose an operation (add, subtract, multiply, divide), and click "Calculate". The result will be automatically simplified and shown as a decimal too.

Can the denominator be zero?

No, division by zero is undefined. The calculator checks for zero denominators and will alert you if found.

What is fraction simplification?

Simplifying a fraction means dividing both numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD) to get the smallest possible numbers. For example, 4/8 simplifies to 1/2.
