Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How is calories burned calculated?

The tool uses MET (Metabolic Equivalent) formula based on your weight, activity type, and duration.

What activities are supported?

Walking, running, cycling, swimming, yoga, weight training, and many more with scientific MET values.

Is this tool free?

Yes, this tool is 100% free to use online with no registration or payment required. No hidden charges.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All processing is done locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server or stored anywhere.

What browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are fully supported. The tool works on both desktop and mobile devices.

Are there usage limits?

No, you can use this tool as many times as you need. There are no daily limits or usage caps.

Does it work offline?

After the page loads, the tool works entirely in your browser. No internet connection is needed for processing.
