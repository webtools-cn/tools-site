📖 Electricity Tips

  • 1 kWh = 1000 watts running for 1 hour — the standard unit on your electricity bill
  • A 1200W AC running 8 hours/day uses about 288 kWh per month
  • Standby power adds up — a TV on standby (~2W) uses ~1.4 kWh/month
  • Each kWh produces roughly 0.5 kg of CO₂ — saving power cuts emissions
  • Tiered pricing means higher rates as usage increases — keep total usage low
