How to Use

Enter the coefficients a, b, c of the quadratic equation ax² + bx + c = 0 and click calculate.

The tool automatically computes the discriminant Δ = b² - 4ac and displays real or complex roots.

All calculations happen in your browser — no data is uploaded to any server.
