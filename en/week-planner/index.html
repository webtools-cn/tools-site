How to Use the Weekly Planner

Converting with the Weekly Planner takes just three steps:

  1. Enter the Value to Convert
    Type or paste the value you want to convert into the input area. The weekly planner supports various input formats — just enter your data and the tool will recognize it automatically.
  2. Select Source and Target Formats
    Choose the source format and the target format from the available options. The tool supports bidirectional conversion where applicable. Your settings are applied instantly.
  3. Get Your Conversion Result
    The converted result appears in real time. Click "Copy" to copy it to your clipboard, or "Download" to save it. All conversion is done locally on your device with no server upload.

FAQ

Why use a weekly planner?

A weekly planner helps visualize your week and distribute workload evenly. Studies show 15 minutes of weekly planning boosts productivity by 25%+ and reduces anxiety and procrastination.

Where is my data stored?

All schedule data is stored in your browser's localStorage, organized by date. Data persists after closing the browser or restarting. Clearing browser cache will erase data.

Can I mark priorities?

Yes! Use colored dots to mark priority: 🔴 High, ⚪ Normal, 🟢 Low. Click the ✓ button to mark tasks as complete. Progress is shown for each day.

Can I view different weeks?

Yes! Use the Previous/Next buttons to navigate between weeks, or pick a specific date to jump to that week. Each week's data is stored independently.
