📖 How to Use

Open multiple URLs in new tabs with one click. Great for SEO analysis, website checks, and competitive research.

Steps: 1) Enter URLs (one per line) 2) Set delay (1-3s recommended) 3) Click Open All.

The tool auto-completes missing http:// or https:// protocols and supports deduplication and validation.

🎯 Use Cases

  • SEO Audits: Open multiple pages to check indexing status
  • Link Validation: Test if URLs are accessible
  • Competitor Research: Open competitor sites for comparison
  • Site Monitoring: Periodically check all site pages

❓ FAQ

Why won't some URLs open?

Browser security may block some pop-ups. Some sites have anti-bot measures. Check URLs for correctness and ensure they use https://.

How to manage many open tabs?

Use tab grouping (right-click → Add to new group) in Chrome/Edge for easy batch management and closing.

What's the delay for?

Delay prevents browser crashes from opening too many tabs at once and avoids triggering rate limits on target sites. 1-3 seconds is recommended.

Are URLs with ports supported?

Yes. Port numbers, paths, query parameters, and anchors are preserved as-is.
