Business Days Calculator - Count Working Days Online

This free online business days calculator helps you quickly determine the number of working days between two dates, excluding weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and custom holidays. Whether you need to calculate project delivery dates, contract fulfillment days, or plan work schedules, this tool provides accurate results instantly.

Key Features

  • Calculate between dates: Enter start and end dates to get the exact count of business days excluding weekends
  • Add business days: Find the future date after adding a specific number of business days
  • Custom holidays: Add company holidays, public holidays, or any dates to exclude
  • Privacy first: All calculation happens in your browser, nothing is uploaded

Use Cases

Project deadline planning, contract delivery date calculation, employee leave day counting, government processing time estimation, exam preparation planning, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the business days calculator?

Select a start date and end date, then click Calculate to get the number of business days between them (excluding weekends). You can also use the 'Add Business Days' mode to find the date after N business days.

Does it include the start date?

Yes. The calculation includes the start date but excludes the end date. For example, Monday to Friday with start on Monday and end on next Monday equals 5 business days.

What counts as a business day?

Monday through Friday, excluding weekends (Saturday and Sunday). You can also add custom holidays to exclude specific dates.

Can I add custom holidays?

Yes. Enter holiday dates in the holidays field, one per line in YYYY-MM-DD format. These will be excluded from the calculation.
