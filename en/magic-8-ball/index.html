🔮 Magic 8 Ball - Classic Fortune-Telling Fun

The Magic 8 Ball is a classic novelty toy invented by Abe Bookman in 1946. Ask a yes/no question, shake the ball, and receive one of 20 possible answers. While it's just for fun, sometimes it helps you make decisions—or at least makes you smile.

How to Use

  1. Type your yes/no question in the box
  2. Click the crystal ball or press "Shake It"
  3. Read the answer that appears
  4. Make your decision—or ask again!

Classic 20 Answers

The Magic 8 Ball contains the classic 20 responses: 5 affirmative, 5 negative, and 10 non-committal. Each shake is completely random.

FAQ

Can the Magic 8 Ball predict the future?

No. It's a fun game with randomly selected answers. For entertainment purposes only—don't use it for life-changing decisions!

How many different answers are there?

20 classic responses including positive, negative, and neutral ones. One appears randomly each time.

Can I shake it without typing a question?

Yes! Just think of your question silently and click the ball. Or use the "Random" button for a preset question.

Is my question history saved?

History is stored in your browser's local storage and is cleared when you close the page or hit "Clear".
