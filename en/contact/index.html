❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install any software?

No. Contact Us is a web-based tool — just open it in your browser and start using it. No downloads, plugins, or extensions required.

Are there any usage limits for Contact Us?

Contact Us has no usage limits — it's free and unlimited. Since processing is done client-side, speed depends on your device. For very large files, we recommend processing in batches for the best experience.

Can I use Contact Us on my phone?

Yes. Contact Us is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The interface adapts automatically to your screen size.

Does Contact Us upload my data to a server?

No. Contact Us uses client-side processing — everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, and the tool works even without an internet connection.

Is my data safe when using Contact Us?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server, so there's zero risk of data leaks. Your information stays completely under your control.
