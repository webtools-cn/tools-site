How to Use

Fill in Your Details Enter your name, contact info, work experience, education, and other details in the form.

Choose a Template Select between Classic and Modern template styles that best suit your industry.

Export Your Resume Use your browser's print function to save as PDF, or preview directly online.

Use Cases

Job Applications：Quickly create or update your resume before applying. Tailor content for each position.

Career Switch：Add new skills and experience when changing careers. Highlight transferable skills.

Internship Applications：Students can create internship resumes highlighting education, projects, and technical skills.

Multiple Versions：Create and manage different resume versions tailored to various job positions and industries.

FAQ

Is my resume data secure?

Absolutely. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your data is stored in localStorage and never uploaded to any server. Data persists across sessions but clearing browser cache will remove it. We recommend exporting backups.

What export formats are supported?

Use your browser's Print function (Ctrl+P / Cmd+P) and select 'Save as PDF' for high-quality PDF export. You can also copy all content with one click. The layout is optimized for A4 printing.

What templates are available?

Two templates: Classic (traditional, good for conservative industries) and Modern (clean with accent colors, great for tech/creative fields). Both are A4-optimized.

Can I add custom sections?

Yes. Toggle sections on/off: Summary, Work Experience, Education, Skills, Projects, Certifications, Languages, Interests. Only enabled sections appear in the preview and export.

Does the resume auto-save?

Yes. Your data auto-saves to browser localStorage as you type. It will be restored when you reopen the page. We recommend exporting a TXT backup as well.
