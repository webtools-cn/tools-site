📖 How to Use

The Reading Time Estimator helps writers, editors, and content creators quickly estimate how long an article will take to read. Here's how:

Step 1: Enter Your Text

Paste your article content into the text box. The tool supports English, Chinese, and mixed-language text. You can also click "Fill Sample" to try it out quickly.

Step 2: Select Language and Speed

Choose the primary language of your text (English/Chinese/Mixed) and the reading speed level you want to see. Different speeds correspond to different words-per-minute rates.

Step 3: View Results

Click "Estimate Reading Time" to get word count, sentence count, paragraph count, and estimated reading time at various speeds.

🎯 Use Cases

  • Blog Writing: Estimate reading time before publishing to help readers decide whether to continue.
  • Speech Preparation: Estimate how long your script will take to read aloud, adjust content length accordingly.
  • Content Marketing: SEO best practices suggest 3-7 minute reading time for articles. This tool helps optimize.
  • Academic Writing: Estimate reading time for each chapter of your thesis or paper.
  • Audiobook Production: Estimate recording duration based on text length to plan production schedules.

💡 Reading Speed Reference

Reading speed varies significantly across different groups and contexts. Here are common reference values:

  • Slow Reading (150 wpm): Intensive reading, studying, or reading in a foreign language.
  • Normal Reading (230 wpm): Average adult reading speed for English. Chinese averages 300-500 characters/min.
  • Fast Reading (320 wpm): Proficient readers skimming through content.
  • Skimming (500 wpm): Scanning headings and key sentences only.

Research shows Chinese reading speed is typically higher than English because Chinese characters carry more information density. A 3,000-character article takes about 10 minutes at normal speed.

❓ FAQ

How is word count calculated for Chinese vs English?

Chinese is counted by characters (each character counts as one word). English is counted by words (separated by spaces). In mixed mode, both are counted separately and summed.

How are reading speeds determined?

We reference multiple reading studies. Average Chinese reading speed is about 300-500 characters/min, and English is about 200-300 words/min. Choose the speed closest to your reading habits.

Is the estimate accurate?

Reading time is influenced by many factors including content difficulty, reader proficiency, and reading purpose. This tool provides a statistical estimate; actual reading time may vary.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. Your text content never leaves your device.
