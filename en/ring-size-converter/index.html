📏 Measurement Guide

  • Wrap a strip of paper or string around the base of your finger
  • Mark where the ends meet, then measure the length (circumference)
  • Find the corresponding size in the chart above
  • Measure at different times (finger size varies with temperature)
  • For engagement rings, choose slightly larger
