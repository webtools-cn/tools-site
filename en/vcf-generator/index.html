FAQ


What is the VCF/vCard generator?
An online tool that creates vCard 3.0 format contact files. Fill in info and download VCF for direct import to phone contacts.


What is a VCF/vCard file?
VCF is the standard electronic business card format containing contact info, recognized by virtually all contact software.


How to import to phone?
iOS: Send VCF via email/AirDrop, tap to import. Android: Open VCF with file manager. Also via Google Contacts web.


Is data uploaded?
No. All contact info is processed locally in your browser. VCF files are generated locally, data never uploaded.


Can I generate multiple contacts?
Currently one contact per VCF file. For bulk, generate multiple and merge, or use CSV import to Google Contacts.
