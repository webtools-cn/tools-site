❓ FAQ

Is the spin truly random?

Yes. The wheel uses physics-based spin simulation with random velocity. Each result is completely unpredictable. Enable 'Turbo Random' for extra randomness.

How many options can I add?

Up to 50 options. We recommend 2-30 for best visuals. Each option gets a unique color.

Is my data uploaded?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Spin the Wheel free to use?

Yes, Spin the Wheel is completely free to use. No registration or login required — all features are available immediately.

Do I need to install any software?

No. Spin the Wheel is a web-based tool — just open it in your browser and start using it. No downloads, plugins, or extensions required.

Is my data safe when using Spin the Wheel?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server, so there's zero risk of data leaks. Your information stays completely under your control.

Can I use Spin the Wheel on my phone?

Yes. Spin the Wheel is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The interface adapts automatically to your screen size.

Does Spin the Wheel upload my data to a server?

No. Spin the Wheel uses client-side processing — everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, and the tool works even without an internet connection.

Are there any usage limits for Spin the Wheel?

Spin the Wheel has no usage limits — it's free and unlimited. Since processing is done client-side, speed depends on your device. For very large files, we recommend processing in batches for the best experience.
