About Online Notepad

A clean, fast online text editor for daily note-taking, drafting, and temporary recording. Content auto-saves to your browser and restores when you return.

Key Features

  • Auto-save: real-time saving to browser LocalStorage
  • Character stats: real-time character, word, and line count
  • Find & replace: search and replace text with case sensitivity
  • Privacy: all data stays local, nothing uploaded
  • Dark theme: matches your system theme for eye comfort

Use Cases

  • Quick idea capture and note-taking
  • Temporary text paste and organization
  • Drafting and outlining
  • Classroom/meeting notes

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Does content auto-save? Will I lose my data?

Yes, content auto-saves to browser LocalStorage. As long as you use the same browser, content persists. Note: 1) Clearing browser data removes LocalStorage; 2) Not shared between different browsers; 3) Private/incognito mode data is lost when closing the tab. Export important content regularly.

❓ What text editing features are supported?

Basic text editing features: 1) Real-time character/word/line count; 2) Find & replace (case-sensitive option); 3) Fullscreen mode; 4) Auto-save; 5) Clear formatting. This tool focuses on clean, lightweight text editing without rich text formatting (bold, italic, etc.).

❓ What is the maximum text capacity?

Theoretically no strict limit, but browser LocalStorage is typically limited to 5-10MB. For plain text, this holds millions of characters. For very large content, save in segments or export as TXT. Extremely large text may affect editor performance.

❓ Can I export to other formats?

Export to plain TXT format is supported. You can also copy content and paste into Word, Google Docs, or other editors for format conversion. For Markdown, PDF, or other formats, please use our dedicated conversion tools.

❓ Is my data uploaded? What about privacy?

This is a purely client-side application. All data is stored locally in your browser. Content is never uploaded to any server or transmitted over the internet. Your privacy is fully protected - this is the key advantage over cloud-based note apps.
