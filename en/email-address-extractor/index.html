Email Address Extractor

📖 How to Use

Paste text containing email addresses (web pages, mailing lists, documents) into the input area and click 'Extract Emails'. The tool automatically identifies and extracts all valid email addresses, showing total count and deduplicated results.

⚡ How It Works

Uses regular expressions to match standard email format: alphanumeric/special chars@domain.extension. Supports major email formats and domain extensions. Built-in deduplication ensures unique results.

💼 Use Cases

Collect email addresses from contact pages; organize mailing list recipients; extract contacts from email backups; data cleaning and organization; extract emails from forums and documents.

❓ FAQ

❓ Do I need to upload data?
No. All extraction is done entirely in your browser. Your text is never uploaded to any server.
❓ What email formats can be recognized?
Standard formats (user@domain.com), all domain extensions (.com/.org/.net/.io/.cn/.edu etc.), emails with dots and hyphens (john.doe@company.com.cn).
❓ Can results be exported?
Yes. Supports CSV format (openable in Excel) and TXT format (one email per line).
❓ Does it auto-deduplicate?
Yes. The tool automatically deduplicates results by default. You can disable this to see all occurrences.
❓ Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free. No registration or payment required.
❓ Does it support batch processing?
Yes. Paste large amounts of text and get all email addresses extracted in milliseconds.
