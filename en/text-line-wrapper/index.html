❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What does the text line wrapper do?
This tool wraps long text at a specified character width. Useful for formatting paragraphs, creating fixed-width layouts, and generating monospaced text.
Can I customize the line width?
Yes. Default is 80 characters. Set any value from 1 to 500 using the slider or number input.
What line break styles are supported?
Three styles: LF (Unix/Linux/macOS), CRLF (Windows), and CR (classic Mac).
Is my text uploaded to a server?
No. All text processing happens entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
Will long English words be cut in the middle?
The tool includes smart word-breaking. It tries to break at word boundaries. Very long words are force-broken when necessary.
Does it support Chinese or mixed text?
Yes. Chinese characters are handled naturally. Mixed Chinese and English text is processed correctly.
