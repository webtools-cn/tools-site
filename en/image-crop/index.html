❓ FAQ

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using HTML5 Canvas. Your image never leaves your device.

What fixed crop ratios are supported?

1:1 (square), 4:3 (standard), 16:9 (widescreen), 3:2 (classic), 9:16 (portrait). Select 'Freeform' for manual adjustment.

Can I crop images into a circle?

Yes! Select 'Circle Crop' for a circular output with transparent background — perfect for avatars.

What's the output size?

The cropped image uses the original resolution of the selected area, maintaining full clarity.
