💡 About This Tool

Free online content improvement tool. Improve your writing with readability analysis, passive voice detection, and smart vocabulary suggestions. Pure frontend, no upload.

All processing is done locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.

How to Use

  1. Enter Information
    Fill in the required information such as content, keywords, or preferences.
  2. Configure & Generate
    Adjust options as needed, then click the generate button. Results appear instantly.
  3. Copy & Use
    Copy results to clipboard. All processing is done locally in your browser.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Content Improver free to use?

Yes, it's completely free with no registration or login required. All features are available with no usage limits.

Does it upload my data?

No. All processing happens in your local browser — no data is uploaded. Works even offline.

What features are supported?

Readability analysis (Flesch score), passive voice detection, complex word suggestions, sentence length analysis, and word repetition detection.

How accurate are suggestions?

The tool uses proven text analysis algorithms. For professional writing, combine with human judgment.

Are there usage limits?

No usage limits or character restrictions. Speed depends on your device performance.
