About Color Picker from Image

This free color picker from image lets you extract exact color values from any uploaded image. Simply move your mouse over the image to preview colors, click to capture them. HEX, RGB, and HSL values are displayed simultaneously.

Use Cases

  • Web design: Extract color schemes from reference images
  • Graphic design: Get exact color codes from brand logos or materials
  • UI design: Pick precise colors from design mockups
  • Photography: Analyze dominant colors and color distribution

FAQ

How do I pick colors from an image?

Upload an image, then move your mouse or click on the image. The tool instantly shows the color values of the pixel at your cursor position in HEX, RGB, and HSL formats.

How accurate is color picking?

Pixel-perfect. The tool reads the exact pixel value at your cursor position, giving you the precise color of any point in the image.

How do I copy color values?

Click on any displayed color value to automatically copy it to your clipboard. All three formats (HEX, RGB, HSL) support one-click copying.

What image formats are supported?

PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, and other common formats. All processing is local — images never leave your device.

Why does the picked color differ from what I expected?

Compressed or processed images may have slightly altered colors. Monitor calibration also affects color display. For best results, use original high-quality images.
