How to Use the Word Frequency Counter

Process text with the Word Frequency Counter in three simple steps:

  1. Enter Your Text
    Type or paste the text you want to process into the input area. The tool supports plain text input and handles various text formats.
  2. Choose the Text Operation
    Select the text processing operation you need — counting, conversion, transformation, or analysis. Configure any additional options.
  3. Get the Result
    The processed text appears instantly. Click "Copy" to copy to your clipboard or "Download" to save. All processing happens locally.

FAQ

What is word frequency analysis used for?

Word frequency analysis is used for SEO keyword analysis, text style analysis, linguistics research, content optimization, and keyword density checking.

How does the word mode work?

In word mode, text is split by whitespace and each word is counted. In character mode, each individual character is counted separately.

How to filter stop words?

Enter words to exclude in the filter field, separated by commas. Common English stop words are pre-filled as defaults.
