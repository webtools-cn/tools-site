This Pregnancy Calculator calculates current week, due date, and progress based on your LMP and cycle length. Track your pregnancy journey.

How to Use

  1. Enter your LMP (Last Menstrual Period) date
  2. Enter your cycle length (default 28 days)
  3. View current week, due date, estimated conception date, and progress bar

Three Trimesters

  • Second Trimester (Weeks 13-27) - Rapid fetal growth
  • Third Trimester (Weeks 28-40) - Fetal maturation and birth preparation
