What Can the Chinese Tax Calculator Do?

The Chinese Tax Calculator is a free online tool that calculates individual income tax based on the latest tax law. Supports salary and year-end bonus calculations. Pure frontend, no data uploads.

Core Features

  • Salary tax: Monthly calculation
  • Year-end bonus: Separate and combined methods
  • Special deductions: Various deduction types
  • Social insurance: Auto-calculate deductions

How to Use

  1. Enter income: Input pre-tax monthly salary
  2. Fill deductions: Enter social insurance and special deductions
  3. Calculate: View after-tax income and breakdown

Use Cases

  • Salary negotiation: Know actual take-home pay
  • Bonus planning: Choose optimal taxation method
  • Job change: Compare after-tax differences

Background

The 2019 tax reform raised the threshold to 5,000 CNY/month and introduced special additional deductions. Seven-level progressive rates from 3% to 45%.

FAQ

What is the tax threshold?

The current threshold is 5,000 CNY per month. Taxable income is the balance after deducting threshold, social insurance, and special deductions.

What are special deductions?

Children education, continuing education, housing loan interest, housing rent, elderly care, and major medical expenses.

How is year-end bonus taxed?

Before end of 2027, bonus can be taxed separately or combined with comprehensive income.

What are the tax rates?

Comprehensive income uses 3%-45% seven-level progressive rates.

Is my data safe?

All calculations run locally in your browser. No income data is uploaded.
