FAQ

Does GIF compression lose quality?

Yes, but you control the trade-off. Start with 128 colors, 1/2 frame skip, and 100% scale — you can typically reduce file size by 30-50% without noticeable quality loss.

What's the maximum file size?

No hard limit, but very large files (over 10MB or 500+ frames) may take significant time and are limited by browser memory.

Will it still be animated?

Yes! The compressed GIF retains animation. Frame skip controls how many frames are kept.

Is my GIF uploaded?

No. This tool is 100% client-side. Your GIF stays in browser memory and is never sent to any server.

Why is the result larger?

If the original GIF is already optimized, re-encoding may not help. Try reducing colors to 64 or 32 and enabling frame skip.

Technical Details

This tool runs 100% in your browser using modern web technologies. No data is ever sent to any server. Your files remain on your device at all times.
