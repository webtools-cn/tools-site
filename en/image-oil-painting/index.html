Frequently Asked Questions

How does the oil painting effect work?

The tool simulates oil painting by applying a specialized filter that replaces each pixel's color with the most common color in its neighborhood, creating brush-like strokes and reducing detail for a painted look.

What do the Brush Size and Detail controls do?

Brush Size controls the size of simulated brush strokes - larger values create broader, more abstract strokes. Detail Level controls how much original detail is preserved - higher values retain more fine details.

What image types work best?

Photos with clear subjects and good contrast work best. Landscape photos, portraits, and still life images all produce great oil painting results.

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free! No registration required, all processing happens in your browser locally.
