What is Currency Conversion?

Currency conversion is the process of exchanging one currency for another at a specific exchange rate. Exchange rates fluctuate based on market supply and demand, economic policies, international trade, and other factors.

Factors Affecting Exchange Rates

Exchange rates are influenced by interest rate differentials, inflation rates, trade balances, political stability, and market expectations. Central bank interventions, international capital flows, and geopolitical events can also have significant impacts.
