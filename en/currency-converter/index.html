About Currency Converter

Free online currency converter. Convert between 40+ world currencies including USD, EUR, CNY, JPY, GBP and more. Select currencies and enter amount for instant conversion. Built-in reference exchange rates (based on USD mid-rate) with last updated timestamp. Supports all major fiat currencies with one-click reverse conversion. Perfect for travel, e-commerce, overseas study, and international money transfer planning. Client-side only, no data upload.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free. No registration, no download required. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

Absolutely not. This is a purely client-side application. All processing happens locally in your browser — your data never leaves your device.

How are exchange rates determined?

Exchange rates are determined by global forex market supply and demand, influenced by central bank base rates. Major rate sources include the European Central Bank (ECB) and International Monetary Fund (IMF).
