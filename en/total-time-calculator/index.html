What Can the Total Time Calculator Do?

The Total Time Calculator is a free online tool designed to add up multiple time durations. Whether you need to track weekly work hours, sum up project durations, or calculate total video lengths, simply enter each duration per row and the calculator automatically computes the total in real-time, displaying results in hours, minutes, seconds, and days.

Key Features

  • Multiple Duration Addition: Add unlimited time durations with automatic summing
  • Flexible Input: Enter hours, minutes, and seconds separately
  • Real-time Calculation: Results update instantly as you type
  • Multi-unit Conversion: Shows total in hours, minutes, seconds, and days simultaneously
  • Example Data: One-click demo to quickly understand the tool
  • Export Results: Copy or download calculation results

How to Use

  1. Enter hours, minutes, and seconds for each time duration, or click "Load Example"
  2. Click "Add Duration" to add more rows as needed
  3. The calculator automatically sums all durations in real-time
  4. Click "Copy Result" or "Download" to save your results

Use Cases

Work Hours Tracking

HR managers or project leads track weekly employee hours. Enter daily work hours and get the weekly total instantly for overtime and payroll calculations.

Project Timing

Freelancers record time spent on each project, then sum up for client billing. Supports precise minute-level tracking.

Study Time Management

Students log daily study hours and sum up weekly or monthly totals to create better study plans.

Technical Notes

Time addition involves carry-over handling across hours and days. For example, 1h 50m + 2h 30m = 4h 20m (minutes exceeding 60 carry to hours). This calculator handles all carry-overs automatically. For work hours tracking, 24-hour format is recommended to avoid AM/PM confusion.

How many time durations can I add?

Unlimited. Keep adding rows and the calculator will automatically sum all durations.

Is my data uploaded to a server?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. No data is uploaded. Works offline too.

What time formats are supported?

Supports separate hours/minutes/seconds input, as well as direct time format like 2:30 or 1:45:30.

Can I use this for work hours tracking?

Absolutely. Enter daily work hours as separate rows and get the total with multiple unit conversions.

Can I export the results?

Yes. Copy to clipboard or download as a text file.
