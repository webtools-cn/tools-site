📖 2026 China Individual Income Tax Guide

This tool is based on the latest China Individual Income Tax Law, using the 7-bracket progressive tax rate system. It accurately calculates IIT for salary income and annual bonuses, with support for cumulative withholding monthly details, special additional deductions, and reverse tax calculation.

IIT Calculation Formula

Tax Payable = Taxable Income \u00d7 Applicable Tax Rate - Quick Deduction

Taxable Income = Annual Income - Basic Deduction (60,000 CNY) - Social Insurance (employee) - Special Additional Deductions

Cumulative Withholding Method

China uses the cumulative withholding method for salary IIT: each month, cumulative income minus cumulative deductions is calculated, annual tax brackets are applied, and previously withheld tax is subtracted. As cumulative income increases monthly, the monthly tax may gradually increase.

Special Additional Deductions (2026)

  • Children Education: 2,000 CNY/month per child
  • Continuing Education: 400 CNY/month (degree) or 3,600 CNY/year (certification)
  • Major Medical Expenses: Up to 80,000 CNY/year
  • Housing Loan Interest: 1,000 CNY/month, up to 240 months
  • Housing Rent: 800-1,500 CNY/month (varies by city tier)
  • Elderly Care: 3,000 CNY/month (full for only child, shared for siblings)
  • Childcare (Under 3): 2,000 CNY/month per child

Annual Bonus Tax Methods

The separate taxation policy for annual bonuses continues in 2026. You can choose: 1) Separate taxation - bonus divided by 12 then applied to monthly brackets; 2) Combined with comprehensive income. This tool automatically compares both and recommends the better option.
