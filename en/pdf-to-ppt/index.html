❓ Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do I need to upload my PDF?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser using PDF.js. Your PDF file never leaves your device. No upload to any server.
❓ What does the converted PPT contain?
Each PDF page becomes a slide in the PowerPoint presentation. Each slide contains an image snapshot of the page to preserve the original layout and formatting.
❓ What format is the output?
The tool generates standard .pptx files compatible with Microsoft PowerPoint 2007+, Google Slides, Apple Keynote, and LibreOffice Impress.
❓ What's the maximum PDF size?
We recommend PDFs under 20MB. Large files with many pages may take longer to process.
❓ Can I edit text in the converted PPT?
The tool renders each PDF page as an image embedded in the PPT, so text in the slides is not directly editable. For editable content, use Adobe Acrobat Pro or professional PDF software.
