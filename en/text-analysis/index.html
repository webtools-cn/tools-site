❓ FAQ

What can the text analyzer do?

The text analyzer counts total characters (with/without spaces), words, lines, paragraphs, and sentences. It estimates reading and speaking time, analyzes word frequency (Top 20), and calculates average word length and sentence length. Perfect for article analysis, SEO optimization, and writing assistance.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is based on average reading speeds: 238 words per minute (WPM) for English and 500 characters per minute for Chinese. Speaking time uses 183 WPM for English and 350 characters per minute for Chinese. These are statistical averages and actual speed varies by individual.

Can it analyze mixed Chinese-English text?

Yes. The tool automatically detects and analyzes mixed Chinese-English text. Chinese characters are counted individually, while English words are separated by spaces. Word frequency analysis captures both Chinese bigrams (2-character combinations) and English words, giving you a comprehensive view of mixed text characteristics.

Is my data sent to any server?

No. All analysis runs locally in your browser. Your text never leaves your device. Pure client-side processing ensures complete privacy. You can safely paste sensitive text for analysis without any security concerns.
