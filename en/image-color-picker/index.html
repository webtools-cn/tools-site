How to Use the Image Color Picker

Process your images with the Image Color Picker in three steps:

  1. Upload Your Image
    Click the upload area or drag and drop an image file. The tool supports common formats including PNG, JPG, GIF, SVG, and WebP. Your image stays on your device at all times.
  2. Configure Image Settings
    Adjust image processing options such as dimensions, quality, filters, or effects. Preview changes in real time before applying them.
  3. Download the Processed Image
    Once you are satisfied with the result, click "Download" to save the processed image. The tool never uploads your images to any server.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the image color picker work?

Upload an image, then click anywhere on the image to pick the color at that pixel. A magnifier shows the area around your cursor for precise selection. The color is displayed in HEX, RGB, and HSL formats.

Can I get the color code for later use?

Yes! Click the Copy HEX, Copy RGB, or Copy HSL buttons to copy the color code to your clipboard. All picked colors are saved in the color history panel.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No! Everything runs in your browser. Your image never leaves your device. All processing is done client-side using Canvas API.

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP, and most other common image formats are supported. The tool works with any image your browser can display.
