Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What can I use this checklist tool for?

You can create any type of checklist: daily to-do lists, shopping lists, travel packing lists, moving checklists, work task lists, exam study checklists, wedding planning checklists, and more. It supports adding items, marking done, deleting, and reordering.

Is my checklist data saved?

Your checklist is saved automatically in your browser's local storage (localStorage). It persists after closing the browser or tab. To permanently save or share your list, use the export function to download it as a text file. Clearing browser cache or using private/incognito mode will delete the data.

How do I export my checklist?

Click the 'Export Text' button to download your checklist as a TXT file. The file includes all items with ✓ for completed and □ for pending items. You can also screenshot or print the page to save your list.

Is there a limit on the number of items?

There is no hard limit. Since data is stored in your browser's local storage (approximately 5MB limit), you can add hundreds or even thousands of items. However, keeping your list manageable is recommended for better usability.

How do I reorder checklist items?

Each item has ↑ up and ↓ down buttons on the right side. Click them to move items up or down. You can also clear the list and re-add items in your desired order.

Is my data secure?

This tool runs entirely in your browser. All data is stored in your device's local storage and is never uploaded to any server. Your checklist data is only visible to you.
