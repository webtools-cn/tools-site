❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What does the audio reverser do?
Reverses audio files so they play backward. Perfect for sound effects, creative music production, and audio analysis.
What formats are supported?
MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A and other common formats. Processed via Web Audio API.
Is my audio uploaded to a server?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Audio data never leaves your device.
Can I save the reversed audio?
Yes. Download as WAV file.
Does reversing reduce audio quality?
No. The process is lossless. Retains original sample rate and bit depth.

This tool processes audio files only. Extract audio from video first.
