FAQ

Q1: What is Neumorphism?

Neumorphism is a UI design style that uses soft backgrounds and dual shadows (light + dark) to create the visual effect of elements "raising" from or "pressing" into the background. It works best with light themes.

Q2: How to choose the right background color?

Background and element colors should be similar. Soft gray-white tones (#e8ecf1) work best. Bright colors weaken the neumorphic effect. Keep element color consistent with background.

Q3: Raised和Pressed有什么区别？

Raised（raised）元素看起来浮在背景之上，适合卡片和按钮的正常状态。Pressed（pressed）元素看起来被压入背景，适合按钮的按下/激活状态。
