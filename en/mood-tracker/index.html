How to Use the Mood Tracker

Generate with the Mood Tracker in three easy steps:

  1. Configure Your Preferences
    Adjust the settings and options for your mood tracker. Choose parameters such as length, complexity, style, or quantity based on your needs.
  2. Click Generate
    Click the "Generate" button to create your content. The tool instantly processes your preferences and produces the result right in your browser — no waiting, no server requests.
  3. Copy or Download the Result
    Review the generated output and click "Copy" to copy it to your clipboard, or "Download" to save it as a file. You can generate as many times as you need with no limits.

FAQ

Why track your mood?

Tracking emotions helps identify patterns and discover what activities, environments, or relationships affect your mood. Long-term tracking increases self-awareness and supports mental health management.

Where is data stored?

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is uploaded to servers. Data persists across sessions but clearing cache will erase it.

Can I view history?

Yes. The tool generates a monthly mood calendar and lists all entries in reverse chronological order for easy review and reflection.

What are the mood levels?

5 levels: 😢 Terrible, 😟 Bad, 😐 Okay, 😊 Good, 🥰 Great. Pick the closest match and optionally add notes about specific events.
