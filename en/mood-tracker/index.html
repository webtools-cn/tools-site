FAQ

Why track your mood?

Tracking emotions helps identify patterns and discover what activities, environments, or relationships affect your mood. Long-term tracking increases self-awareness and supports mental health management.

Where is data stored?

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is uploaded to servers. Data persists across sessions but clearing cache will erase it.

Can I view history?

Yes. The tool generates a monthly mood calendar and lists all entries in reverse chronological order for easy review and reflection.

What are the mood levels?

5 levels: 😢 Terrible, 😟 Bad, 😐 Okay, 😊 Good, 🥰 Great. Pick the closest match and optionally add notes about specific events.
