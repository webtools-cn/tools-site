About Amortization Calculator

Free online amortization calculator using the fixed-rate (equal payment) method. Perfect for mortgages, auto loans, personal loans, and student loans.

How amortization works: Each monthly payment is the same amount. Early payments consist mostly of interest; as the loan matures, more of each payment goes toward principal.
