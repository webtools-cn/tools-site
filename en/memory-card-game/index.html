About Memory Card Game

Free online Memory Card Game. Classic flip-and-match gameplay. 3 difficulty levels (4×3, 4×4, 6×4 grids) with multiple themes (Emoji, Animals, Food, Flags). Tracks flips, matches, and completion time. Challenge your best score! Perfect for kids brain training, memory practice, and family fun.

How to Play

  • Click cards to flip them and find matching pairs
  • Only 2 cards can be flipped at a time
  • Non-matching cards flip back automatically
  • Complete all matches as fast as possible!
