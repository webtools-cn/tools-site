FAQ

What is an RSS Feed?

RSS (Really Simple Syndication) is a web feed format for publishing frequently updated content. Users subscribe to feeds and get updates without visiting each website manually.

RSS 2.0 vs Atom?

RSS 2.0 is the most widely supported format. Atom is a newer alternative with stricter syntax. This tool generates RSS 2.0 format.

Where to put the RSS file?

RSS feeds are typically placed at /feed.xml, /rss.xml, or /feed/ on your website.

How to validate RSS?

Use the W3C Feed Validation Service (validator.w3.org/feed) to validate RSS syntax.
