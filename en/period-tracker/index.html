Frequently Asked Questions

How does the period calculator work?

Based on your last period date and average cycle length, the algorithm predicts your next period, ovulation day (14 days before next period), and fertile window (3 days before and after ovulation).

How accurate is the calculation?

Calculations are estimates based on average cycle length. Actual cycles may vary. This is for reference only, not medical advice.

What if my cycle is irregular?

For irregular cycles, track 3-6 months of data and use the average cycle length.
