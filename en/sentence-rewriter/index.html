How It Works

The Sentence Rewriter helps you quickly rephrase sentences while preserving the original meaning. It supports multiple rewriting modes for different scenarios.

Synonym Replacement: Replaces key words with synonyms, keeping the sentence structure intact. Ideal for light rewriting.

Formal Tone: Converts casual expressions to formal language, suitable for business documents and academic writing.

Casual Tone: Converts formal expressions to everyday language, suitable for social media and casual communication.

Concise Expression: Removes redundant words for a more compact expression.

Expanded Expression: Adds detail and modifiers for richer expression.

FAQ

Does the rewritten text maintain the original meaning?

The tool tries to preserve the original meaning, but deeper rewriting may introduce more variation. We recommend reviewing the result for accuracy.

How is this different from a translation tool?

A rewriter changes the expression within the same language, while a translator converts between languages. Rewriting keeps the language but changes the expression.

Can I rewrite an entire article?

Yes. For best results, process long texts in paragraphs of up to 500 words each.

Can I use the rewritten text for academic writing?

The tool can help adjust expression, but academic writing requires proper citations and originality. Use the result as a reference and reorganize with your own understanding.
