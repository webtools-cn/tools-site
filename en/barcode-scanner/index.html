❓ FAQ

Which barcode formats are supported?

QR Code, EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A, UPC-E, Code-128, Code-39, ITF, Codabar, Data Matrix, PDF417, Aztec and more.

Do I need to install an app?

No. It works directly in your browser — no downloads required.

Is my scanned data safe?

Absolutely. All scanning happens locally in your browser. No data is ever uploaded to any server.
