❓ FAQ

Is the PDF Add Image tool free?

Yes, completely free. No registration required, no usage limits. All processing happens locally in your browser.

What image formats are supported?

PNG and JPG/JPEG formats are supported. Images are embedded directly into the PDF file at original quality.

Can I adjust image size and position?

Yes. You can customize X/Y coordinates, scaling, and opacity. You can also choose to insert images on specific pages or all pages.

Will adding images reduce PDF quality?

No. Images are embedded at original quality without compression or conversion.

Is my file secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
