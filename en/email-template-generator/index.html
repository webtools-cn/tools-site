Frequently Asked Questions

Which email clients are supported?

The template uses inline CSS and table-based layout for maximum compatibility. Works with Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, Thunderbird, Yahoo Mail, and most other email clients. The table layout ensures correct rendering in Outlook.

How do I customize the template?

Select an email type, then fill in your company name, recipient, subject line, and body content. You can freely modify the heading, paragraphs, button text, and footer note. For advanced modifications, edit the generated HTML directly.

Are the templates mobile-responsive?

Yes. All templates include responsive media queries that adapt to mobile screens. The layout collapses to a single column on small screens, and font sizes adjust automatically for optimal mobile reading.
