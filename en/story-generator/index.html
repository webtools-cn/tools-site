How to Use the Story Generator

Creating random short stories takes just three steps:

  1. Pick a Genre
    Choose from Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Mystery, Romance, Horror, or select Random for mixed genres.
  2. Generate
    Click "Generate Story" to create a complete short story with a full narrative arc.
  3. Read & Save
    Read your story, click Copy to save it, or keep generating for fresh inspiration.

FAQ

How long are generated stories?

Each story is 200-500 words with a complete narrative structure: setup, conflict, development, and resolution.

Do stories repeat?

The generator uses template-based random combination, so each story is unique. However, similar plot frameworks may appear over many generations.

What genres are supported?

Currently supports Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Mystery, Romance, Horror, and Random. More genres will be added in future updates.

Can I use these stories commercially?

Use stories as creative inspiration. Your original work based on generated ideas is your own intellectual property.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The tool is fully responsive and works great on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I use it offline?

Yes. All story templates are included in the page. After first load, it works completely offline.
