❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this CSV Formatter safe to use?

Yes. All processing happens in your browser — no data is ever uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy.

What does CSV formatting do?

It auto-aligns CSV columns to equal width, beautifies data layout, and adds visual cues like bold headers and alternating row colors for better readability.

Which delimiters are supported?

Comma, tab, semicolon, and pipe (|) delimiters are supported, with auto-detection or manual selection.

How large a CSV file can it handle?

Since processing is browser-side, it depends on your device. Typically tens of thousands of rows work smoothly.

Can I export the formatted result?

Yes. You can copy the formatted CSV to clipboard or download it as a .csv file.
