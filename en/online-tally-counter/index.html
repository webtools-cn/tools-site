🔢 Online Tally Counter

Click to count with multiple counters, custom steps, and keyboard shortcuts. Auto-saves data.

⌨️ Shortcuts: Space/Enter=+1 | Minus=-1 | R=Reset | 1-9=Select counter

📖 How to Use

Getting Started

Click "New" to create a counter, then use +/- buttons or keyboard shortcuts to count. Data auto-saves in your browser.

Use Cases

  • Count people, items, or inventory
  • Track exercise reps (push-ups, sit-ups)
  • Habit tracking and streak counting
  • Inventory management
  • Experiment data recording

📖 FAQ

Will my counter data be lost?

No. Counter data is automatically saved in your browser's localStorage. Even if you refresh or close the browser, your data will be there next time. Clearing browser cache will delete the data.

Can I create multiple counters?

Yes. You can create multiple independent counters, each with its own name and value. Perfect for tracking multiple items simultaneously.

Are keyboard shortcuts supported?

Yes. Press Space or Enter to +1, minus key to -1, and R to reset the active counter. Shortcuts make counting much faster.

Can I customize the step size?

Yes. You can set a custom increment/decrement step for each counter. Default is 1, but you can set 5, 10, 100, or any value you need.

Is my data uploaded to a server?

No. All counter data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your privacy is fully protected.
