Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a meme?

Choose a built-in template or upload your own image (JPG/PNG). Enter top and bottom text, adjust font size and color, then click 'Generate Meme'. Supports text position adjustment. Download with one click.

What image formats are supported?

Supports JPG, PNG, and GIF uploads. Images above 800×800 pixels are recommended for best results. Generated memes are saved as PNG format with high quality.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All image processing and text overlay happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API. Images are never uploaded to any server. Safe for commercial use.
