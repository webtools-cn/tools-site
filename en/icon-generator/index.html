About Icon Generator

This free online icon generator helps you generate various standard-sized application icons and website favicons from a single source image. It covers common sizes for Favicon, iOS app icons, Android icons, and social sharing icons.

Supported Icon Sizes

  • Favicon: 16×16, 32×32, 48×48, 64×64 (website bookmarks)
  • iOS: 60×60, 76×76, 120×120, 152×152, 167×167, 180×180 (iPhone/iPad)
  • Android: 48×48, 72×72, 96×96, 144×144, 192×192, 512×512 (Google Play)
  • Social: 150×150 (search snippets), 256×256 (universal HD)

Tips

  • Upload at least 512×512 pixels for best results
  • PNG images with transparent backgrounds work best
  • Square images produce the best-looking icons

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What types of icons can this tool generate?

It can generate multiple icon sizes from an uploaded image: Favicons (16-64px), iOS app icons, Android icons, and social sharing icons. All sizes can be downloaded individually or in a batch.

How good is the icon quality?

Icons are rendered via Canvas, preserving the original image quality. Upload at least 512x512 pixels for best results. Small sizes (like 16px) use anti-aliasing for clarity.

No. All processing is done entirely in your browser using HTML5 Canvas. Your images never leave your device, keeping your design assets secure.

What image formats are supported?

PNG, JPG, JPEG, and WebP formats are supported. PNG is recommended as it supports transparent backgrounds for better icon rendering.

Can I customize icon sizes?

The tool includes preset sizes covering iOS, Android, and Web platform standards. You can generate all standard sizes at once to cover every use case.
