FAQ

Q1: What statistics are supported?

Numeric columns: count, mean, median, mode, standard deviation, variance, min, max, range, Q1/Q3 quartiles, IQR, skewness, kurtosis, sum. Text columns: count, unique values, most common, least common, missing values.

Q2: Is my data uploaded to a server?

No! All statistical calculations are performed locally in your browser. Data is never uploaded to any server, ensuring privacy and security.

Q3: How are missing values handled?

Empty cells and unparseable values are automatically identified as missing values and excluded from calculations. The count of missing values is displayed in results to help assess data quality.
