FAQ

What is an anagram?

An anagram is formed by rearranging the letters of a word or phrase to create a new word or phrase. For example, 'listen' to 'silent', 'cinema' to 'iceman'.

How to use this anagram finder?

Enter letters or a word in the input box and click 'Find Anagrams'. The tool searches its dictionary for all possible letter rearrangements.

Does it support Chinese?

This tool primarily supports English anagrams with its built-in dictionary. Chinese pinyin can be used indirectly.

What are anagrams used for?

Anagrams are used in word games (Scrabble), crossword puzzles, creative writing, cryptography education, and vocabulary building.
