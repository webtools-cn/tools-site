FAQ

What is NATO phonetic alphabet?

A standardized alphabet assigning code words to letters (A=Alpha, B=Bravo, etc.) for clear radio and telephone communication.

How are numbers represented?

0-9 map to Zero, One, Two, Three, Four, Five, Six, Seven, Eight, Niner.

Is my data safe?

Completely safe. All conversion happens locally in your browser.
