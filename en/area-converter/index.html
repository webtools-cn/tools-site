📖 Frequently Asked Questions

How many square meters are in an acre?

1 acre equals approximately 4,046.86 square meters. The exact conversion is 1 acre = 4,046.8564224 square meters. Conversely, 1 square meter ≈ 0.000247 acres. This converter supports real-time conversion between all major area units.

How do you convert hectares to acres?

1 hectare equals approximately 2.471 acres. To convert hectares to acres, multiply the hectare value by 2.471. For example, 10 hectares = 10 × 2.471 = 24.71 acres. To convert acres to hectares, divide the acre value by 2.471.

What's the difference between square feet and square meters?

1 square foot (sq ft) equals approximately 0.0929 square meters. Square feet are commonly used in the US, Canada, and the UK for real estate and interior design, while square meters are the standard unit in most other countries. 1,000 sq ft ≈ 92.9 square meters.

How many square feet are in an acre?

1 acre equals 43,560 square feet. This is a standard conversion used in US real estate and land measurement. Half an acre is 21,780 sq ft, and a quarter acre is 10,890 sq ft. This tool can instantly convert between acres and square feet.

How do you calculate square kilometers to hectares?

1 square kilometer (km²) equals 100 hectares. To convert, multiply square kilometers by 100. For example, 5 km² = 500 hectares. This is useful for measuring large land areas, national parks, and agricultural regions.
