📖 How to Use

Device Mockup places your screenshot inside a realistic device frame for professional presentation. Perfect for:

  • App Store / Google Play submission screenshots
  • UI/UX design portfolio presentations
  • Product demos and marketing materials
  • Web design case studies

Your screenshot is automatically fitted to the device screen. Adjust zoom level and background color as needed.

❓ FAQ

What image formats are supported?

PNG, JPEG/JPG, and WebP formats are supported. PNG is recommended for best quality. The exported mockup image is in PNG format.

Are there size requirements for screenshots?

No strict requirements. The tool automatically fits your screenshot into the device screen area. For best results, use native device resolution screenshots (e.g., 1179×2556 for iPhone 15).

Is my image secure?

All processing is done locally in your browser using the Canvas API. No images are uploaded to any server. You can use this tool completely offline.
