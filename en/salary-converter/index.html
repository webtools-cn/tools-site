📖 About Salary Conversion

The Salary Converter helps you quickly convert between different salary types, useful for:

  • Job Negotiation: Compare annual salary and hourly rate positions
  • Freelancing: Convert project rates to equivalent monthly or annual salary
  • Cross-country Comparison: Different countries commonly use different salary expressions
  • Budget Planning: Calculate your actual take-home pay after taxes

Note: Conversion results are for reference only. Actual salary is subject to your employment contract. The tax rate setting is only an estimate — please consult your local tax authority for actual tax obligations.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert annual salary to monthly salary?

Monthly salary = Annual salary / 12. You can adjust the number of working months per year based on your actual situation.

How is the hourly rate calculated?

Hourly rate = Annual salary / (Hours per week × Weeks per year). The default assumes 40 hours per week and 52 weeks per year, totaling 2,080 hours.

What is the difference between gross and net salary?

Gross salary is the total income before deductions. Net salary (take-home pay) is the amount after deducting income tax, social security, and other withholdings. Set a combined tax rate to see estimated net salary.

Is my data uploaded to any server?

All calculations are performed locally in your browser. Your salary data is never uploaded to any server and the tool works offline.
