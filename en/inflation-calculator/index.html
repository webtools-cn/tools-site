❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Inflation Calculator free?

Yes, completely free. No registration required.

How does inflation affect purchasing power?

Inflation causes prices to rise, reducing what your money can buy over time. For example, at 3% annual inflation, $100 today will be worth about $74 in 10 years.

What inflation rate should I use?

Refer to official CPI data from your country's statistics bureau. Typical rates range from 1-5%. You can compare multiple scenarios using the comparison section.

Does this tool upload my data?

No. All calculations are done locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server.

Can I calculate for long periods?

Yes. You can calculate up to 100 years. The tool shows a year-by-year breakdown of purchasing power changes.

How is future purchasing power calculated?

Formula: Future Value = Current Amount × (1 - Inflation Rate)^Years. This shows what your money will be worth in terms of today's purchasing power.
