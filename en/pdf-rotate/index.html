📖 How to Use

Free online PDF rotation tool. Fix page orientation in scanned documents or adjust page direction.

Steps: 1) Upload PDF 2) Select rotation angle 3) Choose pages 4) Download result.

🎯 Use Cases

  • Scan Fix: Correct sideways scanned documents
  • Portrait/Landscape: Unify page orientation for reading
  • Print Prep: Ensure correct orientation before printing

❓ FAQ

Does rotation affect quality?

No. Rotation only changes orientation without modifying content — no quality loss.

Can I rotate only some pages?

Yes. Choose 'Select Pages' mode and click the thumbnails you want to rotate.

What angles are available?

90° clockwise, 180°, and 270° (90° counter-clockwise).

Is it safe?

Yes. All processing is local. Files never leave your device.
