Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?
Yes, 100% free with no hidden charges and no usage limits.
Is my data uploaded?
No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
What delimiters are supported?
Comma (,), Tab, semicolon (;), and pipe (|). The tool auto-detects the most likely delimiter.
Can I customize alignment?
Yes. Set left, center, or right alignment per column. Default is left-aligned for all columns.
Does it handle quoted CSV?
Yes. Fields with double quotes, including embedded delimiters and line breaks, are handled automatically.
