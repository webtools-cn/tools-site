📖 FAQ

What's the difference between a letter counter and a character counter?

A character counter counts all characters including spaces, punctuation, digits, and line breaks. A letter counter specifically counts only alphabetic characters (A-Z/a-z), and provides detailed breakdowns like uppercase vs lowercase, vowels vs consonants. Use a letter counter when you need to analyze the letter composition of your text — such as vowel-to-consonant ratio or capitalization frequency. Use a character counter when you just need the total length.

Which letters are vowels and which are consonants?

In English, the vowels are A, E, I, O, U (and their lowercase forms a, e, i, o, u). The letter Y can sometimes function as a vowel, but our tool counts it as a consonant by default. Consonants are all other English letters: B, C, D, F, G, H, J, K, L, M, N, P, Q, R, S, T, V, W, X, Y, Z. The tool provides separate counts for vowels and consonants, which is useful for language learners, writers, and poets.

What can I use the letter counter for?

Common use cases include: controlling letter count and vowel proportion in writing, analyzing keyword density and letter composition for SEO, studying word structures in language learning, checking uppercase usage in editing and proofreading, analyzing text characteristics in academic research, controlling letter patterns in poetry, checking letter proportions for social media posts, and ensuring readability standards in content creation.

How do I interpret the statistics?

The tool shows: Total Letters (A-Z/a-z only, excluding spaces and punctuation), Uppercase Letters (capital letters), Lowercase Letters, Vowels (A/E/I/O/U), Consonants (all other letters), Digits (0-9), Spaces, Punctuation marks (. , ! ? ; : ' \" - ( ) etc.), and Total Characters (everything including spaces and punctuation). Each metric is independently counted so you can understand your text's exact composition.

Is my text data safe? Do you upload it to a server?

Your text never leaves your device. This tool runs entirely in your browser — all counting calculations happen locally. Nothing is uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any server. You can even use it offline. We do not collect, store, or transfer any of your text content. Your data privacy is completely protected.
