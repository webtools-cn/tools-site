📖 FAQ

What is font pairing?

Font pairing combines two or more fonts, typically one for headings (display font) and another for body text (UI font). Good font pairing creates visual contrast and hierarchy while maintaining overall harmony.Common strategies include: serif+Noneserif, bold+light, large size+small size。

How to choose good font pairings?

Font pairing principles: 1) Contrast: heading and body fonts should be clearly different; 2) Harmony: both fonts should coordinate in overall style; 3) Readability: body font must be easy to read; 4) Context: choose pairings that match your design style. Classic pairs include Playfair Display + Source Sans Pro, Montserrat + Merriweather.
