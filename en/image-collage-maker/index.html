How to Make a Photo Collage

  1. Upload Images
    Click empty cells to upload images. Up to 6 images. Click × to remove.
  2. Customize Layout
    Adjust rows, columns, gap spacing, corner radius, and background color.
  3. Download
    Choose output size and download your collage as PNG.

FAQ

How many images can I combine?

Up to 6 images. Grid layouts range from 1×1 to 3×3. Only cells with images are included.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No. All processing is done locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

What are the output dimensions?

Choose Small (300px/cell), Medium (600px/cell), or Large (1000px/cell). Final size = (cell size × columns + gap × (columns-1)).
