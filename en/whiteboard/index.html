❓ FAQ

What is an online whiteboard?

An online whiteboard is a free browser-based drawing tool that supports pen, shapes, text, eraser, and more. No software installation needed, just open and use.

Where is the whiteboard data saved?

All drawing data is saved locally in your browser and never uploaded to any server. You can export your work as PNG or SVG files to save locally.

What drawing tools are supported?

Supports pen, line, rectangle, circle/ellipse, text, eraser, as well as color selection, line width adjustment, undo/redo, and more.

How to add text?

Select the text tool, click on the canvas where you want to place text, enter your text in the prompt, and press Enter to confirm.
