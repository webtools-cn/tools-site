📖 How to Use the Webcam Test Tool

  1. Click "Start Webcam Test" — Your browser will prompt for camera access. Click Allow.
  2. View live video feed — Your webcam feed will appear in the preview area with real-time parameters.
  3. Check test results — The tool automatically detects resolution, FPS, brightness, and contrast.
  4. Take a snapshot — Click "Take Screenshot" to save the current frame as an image.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the webcam test tool free?

Yes, it's completely free to use with no registration or login required. All features are available with no usage limits whatsoever.

Will my camera data be uploaded to any server?

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser locally. All video data stays on your device and is never uploaded to any server. Your privacy is fully protected.

Why does the browser ask for camera permission?

The tool needs access to your camera to test its functionality. Your browser will show a permission prompt — simply click "Allow". If you accidentally denied it, you can change the permission in your browser's site settings by clicking the 🔒 or info icon in the address bar.

How do I interpret the test results?

Results are color-coded: Green means the parameter passes (normal working condition), Yellow means it needs attention, and Red indicates an issue. If all indicators are green, your webcam is working perfectly.

Why is my camera showing a black screen?

Possible reasons: 1) Camera is being used by another app (Zoom, Skype, etc.) — close those apps first; 2) Camera permission was denied — check browser site settings; 3) Hardware issue — check your device manager or System Preferences for the camera status.
