❓ FAQ

Is the video compressor free?▼

Yes, completely free to use. No registration, no login required. All features are available directly. No file size limits, no watermarks.

Are video files uploaded to a server?▼

No. All video compression is done locally in your browser. Files are never uploaded to any server, fully protecting your privacy. Works even offline.

What video formats are supported?▼

Supports importing MP4, WebM, MOV, AVI and other common video formats. Output format depends on browser support, typically WebM (VP9) or MP4 (H.264).

Will video quality decrease after compression?▼

Video compression is lossy, so quality will decrease somewhat. You can balance file size and quality using the compression quality slider. We recommend starting with 0.6-0.8 quality.

What if compression is slow?▼

Compression speed depends on video size and your computer performance. Try lowering the resolution or trimming the video to speed things up. Videos under 5 minutes work best.
