FAQ

What are well-known ports?

Well-known ports (0-1023) are assigned by IANA for standard network services. They typically require administrative privileges to use.

What's the difference between TCP and UDP?

TCP provides reliable, connection-oriented communication. UDP provides fast, connectionless communication. Many services support both protocols.

What are Registered and Dynamic ports?

Registered ports (1024-49151) are registered by IANA for specific services. Dynamic/Private ports (49152-65535) are for temporary use.
