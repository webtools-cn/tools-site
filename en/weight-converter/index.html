❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert kg to pounds?
1 kg = 2.20462 lb. Formula: kg × 2.20462 = lb; lb ÷ 2.20462 = kg. Example: 70 kg ≈ 154.32 lb, 180 lb ≈ 81.65 kg.
How do I convert ounces to grams?
1 oz = 28.3495 g. Formula: oz × 28.3495 = g; g ÷ 28.3495 = oz. Example: 16 oz (1 lb) ≈ 453.6 g, 100 g ≈ 3.53 oz.
What is a stone (st) unit?
Stone (st) is a British imperial weight unit used mainly in the UK and Ireland for body weight. 1 stone = 14 lb ≈ 6.35 kg. Still widely used in UK fitness and health contexts.
How many kg in a ton?
1 metric ton = 1000 kg. US short ton = 2000 lb ≈ 907.2 kg. UK long ton = 2240 lb ≈ 1016 kg. This tool uses metric ton by default.
How accurate is this weight converter?
This tool uses standard international conversion factors with precision to 6 decimal places. More than sufficient for daily use (body weight, cooking, fitness). For scientific research, use professional metrology software.
