About Music Chord Generator

Free online music chord progression generator. Create chord progressions in various styles: Pop, Rock, Jazz, Blues, Classical, and more. Choose major/minor keys, customize tempo. Play chords instantly via Web Audio API. Auto-loop mode. Perfect for songwriters, guitarists, pianists seeking inspiration. Client-side only.

How to Use

  • Select music style and key, click Generate for a chord progression
  • Click Play to hear chords (requires Web Audio API support)
  • Adjust chord count for longer progressions
  • Great for songwriting, improvisation practice, and music education
