❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Fraction to Decimal Calculator free?

Yes, it's completely free to use. No registration or login required.

How to convert a fraction to a decimal?

Simply divide the numerator by the denominator. For example, 3/4 = 3 ÷ 4 = 0.75. This tool does this automatically and shows the step-by-step calculation.

Does this tool upload my data?

No. All calculations happen in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.

What are repeating decimals?

Repeating decimals have a repeating pattern of digits, like 1/3 = 0.333... (repeating 3) or 1/7 = 0.142857142857... (repeating 142857). This tool identifies and marks repeating decimals.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes. The tool is fully responsive and works on all devices.

How to Use

  1. Select direction: Fraction → Decimal or Decimal → Fraction
  2. Fraction → Decimal: enter numerator and denominator
  3. Decimal → Fraction: enter a decimal number (e.g., 0.75)
  4. Click "Calculate" to get the result

Fraction & Decimal Conversion

Fraction to Decimal: Numerator ÷ Denominator = Decimal. Example: 3/4 = 0.75

Decimal to Fraction: Convert decimal to simplest fraction. Example: 0.75 = 75/100 = 3/4

Supports proper fractions, improper fractions, and mixed numbers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate is the conversion?

For rational numbers (finite decimals), results are exact. For repeating decimals, the repeating pattern is identified. Results show up to 10 decimal places.

Q: How to convert mixed numbers?

For a mixed number like 1½, enter numerator as 3 (1×2+1) and denominator as 2. Or use decimal mode and enter 1.5.

Q: Does it simplify fractions?

Yes. When converting decimal to fraction, the result is automatically reduced to its simplest form (e.g., 0.5 → 1/2, not 2/4).

Q: Can denominator be zero?

No. Division by zero is undefined. The tool will show an error message if the denominator is 0.

Q: Does it work offline?

Yes. After loading, all calculations work offline since everything runs in your browser.
