💡 How to Use

  • Enter the roof footprint length and width
  • Select the roof pitch (steeper = higher factor)
  • Choose the roofing material type
  • Enter coverage area per unit of material
  • Set a waste factor (typically 10%)
  • Click "Calculate" for results

📐 Pitch Factors

  • Flat (0°) — Factor 1.00
  • 4:12 — Factor 1.12
  • 6:12 — Factor 1.20 (most common)
  • 8:12 — Factor 1.30
  • 12:12 (45°) — Factor 1.56

📋 Formula

  • Footprint = length × width
  • Roof area = footprint × pitch factor
  • With waste = roof area × (1 + waste%)
  • Units = ⌈with waste ÷ coverage per unit⌉
