❓ FAQ

What is AVIF format?

AVIF (AV1 Image File Format) is a next-gen image format based on AV1 video codec. Compared to JPEG and WebP, AVIF offers higher compression and better quality, reducing file size by over 50%. Chrome and Firefox support it natively.

How do I convert AVIF images?

Upload AVIF, PNG, JPEG, or WebP images, select the target format, adjust quality (1-100), and click Convert. All processing happens locally in your browser—nothing is uploaded.

Which is better, AVIF or WebP?

AVIF typically achieves 20-30% better compression than WebP and supports HDR and wider color gamut. However, WebP has better browser compatibility and faster encoding. Choose based on your target platform.

Does it support batch conversion?

Yes! Select multiple images and the tool converts them sequentially with a bulk download option. Due to client-side processing, we recommend keeping batches under 20 images.
