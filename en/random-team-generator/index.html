About Random Team Generator

Random team generator helps you fairly and randomly assign people into balanced teams. Supports grouping by team count or by members per team.

Use Cases

  • Classroom group discussions/projects
  • Team building activities
  • Sports team assignments
  • Work group assignments
  • Competition team forming

Fairness

Uses cryptographically secure random numbers for truly random, unbiased grouping. Team sizes differ by at most 1 person when uneven division is unavoidable.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How to create random teams?

Steps: 1) Enter all member names in the input area (one per line); 2) Choose grouping method (by number of teams or by members per team); 3) Set team count or members per team; 4) Click 'Generate Teams' to randomly assign members. Generate multiple times for different results.

❓ Is the grouping truly random and fair?

Yes. Uses Web Crypto API (crypto.getRandomValues()) for cryptographically secure random numbers, ensuring fair and unbiased grouping. The tool also displays team size statistics for fairness verification.

❓ Can I group by team count or members per team?

Both modes supported: 1) By team count: specify desired number of teams; 2) By members per team: specify how many per team. Both modes balance team sizes as evenly as possible (max 1 person difference when uneven).

❓ Can I reshuffle after grouping?

Yes. Click 'Generate Teams' again for completely new groupings. You can also manually move members between teams by clicking their names. Results can be copied with one click for sharing.

❓ What is the maximum number of members?

Supports at least 500 members. Grouping is fast even with large lists (pure client-side computation). For over 500 members, process in batches for best experience.
