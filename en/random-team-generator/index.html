❓ FAQ

Is the randomization truly random?

Yes. This tool uses the Fisher-Yates shuffle algorithm to randomize the name list, then distributes names sequentially to ensure fair, unbiased team allocation. Each click of Randomize produces a different result.

What if members can't be divided evenly?

The system distributes extra members one per group, ensuring no group has more than 1 member difference. For example, 10 members into 3 groups results in 4+3+3.

Can I export the results?

Yes! Click the Export button to copy the formatted results to your clipboard, ready to paste into documents or chat apps.

Is my data uploaded to any server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. The names and team assignments never leave your device.
