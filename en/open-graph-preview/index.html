Validate your content with the Open Graph Preview & Tester in three steps:

FAQ

What are Open Graph tags?

Open Graph (OG) is a protocol created by Facebook that uses meta tags to control how web pages appear when shared on social media, including title, description, and image. Twitter Cards use similar tags.

Why is my share preview incorrect?

Social platforms cache OG tag information. If you've updated tags but the preview hasn't changed, use Facebook Sharing Debugger or Twitter Card Validator to force a cache refresh.

What is the recommended image size?

Facebook recommends 1200×630px, Twitter large card 1200×600px, LinkedIn 1200×627px. We recommend at least 1200×630px for compatibility across all platforms.
