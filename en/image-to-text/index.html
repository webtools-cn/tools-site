📖 How to Use

Online OCR tool using Tesseract.js engine to extract text from images. Client-side, no upload.

Steps: 1) Upload image 2) Select language 3) Click Recognize 4) Copy extracted text.

Tips

Clear, high-contrast printed text works best. Handwriting and low-res images may reduce accuracy.

🎯 Use Cases

  • Document Digitization: Extract text from scans into editable format
  • Screenshot to Text: Quickly extract text from screenshots
  • Business Cards: Extract contact info from cards
  • Data Entry: Convert paper documents to digital text

❓ FAQ

How accurate is the OCR?

Over 90% for clear printed text. Handwriting and blurry images will have lower accuracy. Use clear images for best results.

Does it support handwriting?

Tesseract.js works best with printed text. Handwriting accuracy is lower, especially for Chinese.

Can I paste screenshots?

Yes. Press Ctrl+V on the page to paste clipboard screenshots.

Is it safe?

Completely safe. All processing is local. Images are never uploaded.
