FAQ

What is placeholder text?

Placeholder text is temporary content used in design and typesetting to fill space, allowing designers to preview the final layout without being distracted by actual content.

How is this different from Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is Latin-based filler text best suited for English layouts. This tool also provides Chinese and Japanese dummy text for better representation of Asian language layouts.

What output formats are supported?

Plain text paragraphs, HTML paragraph tags, and Markdown format are supported for easy pasting into any editor.
