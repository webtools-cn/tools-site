Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

Our image tools support PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, BMP, SVG, ICO, and TIFF formats. You can convert between any of these formats.

Is there a file size limit for image processing?

Since all processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's memory. Most tools handle images up to 20-50MB without issues.

Will my images be uploaded to a server?

No. All image processing happens locally in your browser using Canvas API and WebAssembly. Your images never leave your device.

Can I batch process multiple images?

Yes, many of our image tools support batch processing. You can upload multiple files and process them all at once.
