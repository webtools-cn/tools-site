Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the health calculators?

Our health calculators use standard medical formulas (e.g., Mifflin-St Jeor for calories, standard BMI formula). They provide good estimates but should not replace professional medical advice.

Should I use these for medical decisions?

These tools provide estimates for informational purposes. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical decisions.
