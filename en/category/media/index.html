Frequently Asked Questions

What audio/video formats are supported?

Our media tools support MP3, WAV, OGG, MP4, WebM, and other common audio and video formats.

Is there a file size limit for media processing?

Since processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device. Most tools handle files up to 100MB. For larger files, we recommend desktop software.

Will my media files be uploaded to a server?

No. All media processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
