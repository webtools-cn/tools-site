Dimension Analysis

Personality Type Test Guide

Personality type tests help you understand your thinking style, behavioral preferences, and interaction patterns. Based on psychological type theory, the test analyzes four dimensions of personality to help you better understand yourself and others.

Four Dimensions

  • Extraversion (E) vs Introversion (I) - Energy source and social preference
  • Sensing (S) vs iNtuition (N) - Information processing style
  • Thinking (T) vs Feeling (F) - Decision-making approach
  • Judging (J) vs Perceiving (P) - Lifestyle preference

FAQ

What is the scientific basis of this test?

This test is based on Jungian psychological type theory and the MBTI framework, analyzing personality from 4 dimensions: Extraversion/Introversion (E/I), Sensing/iNtuition (S/N), Thinking/Feeling (T/F), Judging/Perceiving (J/P). This is a widely used personality analysis framework in psychology.

Is the test result accurate?

Results are based on your responses and reflect your current self-perception. Personality is complex and can change, so results are for reference and self-exploration only. We recommend taking the test multiple times and combining insights with real-life experience.

How long does the test take?

The test contains 12 multiple-choice questions and typically takes 3-5 minutes. Each question describes a scenario - choose the option that best matches your true feelings. There are no right or wrong answers, go with your first instinct.

Will my data be collected?

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server. Your answers and results remain entirely under your control.
