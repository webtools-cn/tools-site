❓ Statistics Terms Explained

TermDescription
MeanAverage of all values. Sum divided by count.
MedianMiddle value when sorted. Not affected by outliers.
ModeMost frequent value(s). May have multiple or none.
Std DeviationMeasures data spread. Higher = more dispersed.
IQRInterquartile Range (Q3-Q1). Middle 50% data spread.
SkewnessAsymmetry measure. Positive = right-skewed, negative = left-skewed.
KurtosisTail heaviness. Higher = more outliers, lower = flatter.
