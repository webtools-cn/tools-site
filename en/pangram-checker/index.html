📖 What is a Pangram?

  • A pangram is a sentence that contains every letter of the alphabet at least once
  • The most famous pangram: "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog"
  • Pangrams are commonly used for font display and keyboard testing
  • A perfect pangram (each letter exactly once) is nearly impossible in English
