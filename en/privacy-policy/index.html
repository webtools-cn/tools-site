1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this Site, our servers and third-party services may automatically collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Browser type and version, operating system, screen resolution

  • Usage Data: Visit time, pages visited, duration of stay, click behavior

1.2 Tool Usage Data

All tools on this Site are implemented as pure front-end applications. Your data (such as text input, uploaded files, etc.) is processed entirely within your browser locally and is never uploaded to our servers. We have no access to and do not collect any content you process while using our tools.

This Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that are used to:

  • Remember your language preference settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized advertising experiences
  • Improve website functionality and performance

2. Google Analytics

This Site uses Google Analytics (Tracking ID: G-9W1157EBQV) to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects the following information through cookies:

This information is processed in an anonymous and aggregated form and does not identify you personally. You can opt out of being tracked by Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or by visiting the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page to opt out of cookie use by multiple third-party vendors.

Cookies used on this Site are categorized as follows:

Most browsers allow you to control cookies, including accepting or rejecting cookies and receiving notifications when a cookie is set. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of this Site.
