Voice Changer Online - Real-Time Audio Effects Tool

The online voice changer is a real-time voice modification tool based on the Web Audio API. No software installation needed - just open your browser and start. Capture microphone input and apply various voice effects instantly to make your voice fun, mysterious, or professional.

Key Features

  • Real-time processing - Zero latency, hear effects as you speak
  • 9 preset effects - Robot, echo, deep, high-pitch, tremolo, underwater, telephone, alien
  • Parameter fine-tuning - Adjust pitch and gain freely
  • Record & download - Record modified voice and download audio file
  • Privacy first - Client-side processing, audio never uploaded

Use Cases

  • Gaming voice chat - Add fun with voice effects
  • Video dubbing - Add character voices
  • Podcast production - Enrich voice expression
  • Privacy protection - Disguise your real voice

FAQ

What is the online voice changer?

A real-time voice modification tool based on Web Audio API. It captures microphone input and applies various voice effects like robot, echo, deep, high-pitch, and more. All processing happens locally in your browser.

What voice effects are supported?

8 voice effects: robot, echo, deep, high-pitch, tremolo, underwater, telephone, and alien. Each effect can be further customized with pitch and gain parameters.

Does the voice changer upload my audio?

No. This tool uses the Web Audio API to process all audio locally. Your voice data is never uploaded to any server. Microphone input is processed only on your device.

Why is there no sound from my microphone?

Ensure: 1) Browser has microphone permission; 2) Microphone device works; 3) System volume is not muted; 4) Using Chrome/Firefox/Edge.

Can I record the changed voice?

Yes. Click the record button to start, click again to stop, then download the audio file.
