❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert feet to meters?
1 ft = 0.3048 m. Formula: ft × 0.3048 = m; m ÷ 0.3048 = ft. Example: 10 ft ≈ 3.05 m, 5 m ≈ 16.4 ft.
How do I convert inches to cm?
1 in = 2.54 cm. Formula: in × 2.54 = cm; cm ÷ 2.54 = in. Example: 12 in (1 ft) ≈ 30.48 cm, 50 cm ≈ 19.69 in.
How do I convert miles to km?
1 mi = 1.60934 km. Formula: mi × 1.60934 = km; km ÷ 1.60934 = mi. Example: 5 mi ≈ 8.05 km, 10 km ≈ 6.21 mi.
How many meters in a yard?
1 yd = 0.9144 m = 3 ft = 36 in. Yards are commonly used in American football field measurements and fabric lengths.
How accurate is this length converter?
This tool uses standard international conversion factors with precision to 6 decimal places. Suitable for daily use including construction, travel, sports, and education.
