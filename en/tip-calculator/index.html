📖 How to Use

Using this tip calculator is straightforward. Just follow these simple steps:

1. Enter the Bill Amount

Enter your total bill amount in the "Bill Amount" field. Decimal values are supported for precise calculations.

2. Choose a Tip Percentage

Use the slider to adjust the tip percentage (0-50%), or click one of the preset buttons (10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, 25%) for quick selection.

3. Set the Number of People

If you are splitting the bill, set the number of people in your party. The calculator will automatically divide both the bill and tip equally.

4. Review the Results

Click "Calculate Tip" to see the bill amount, tip amount, total, and per-person payment. Enable "Round up total" for a clean cash-friendly total.

5. Use Quick Presets

Click any scenario card (Fast Food, Casual Dining, Fine Dining, etc.) to instantly apply the recommended tip rate for that situation.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a standard tip percentage in restaurants?

In the United States, the standard restaurant tip is 15-20% of the pre-tax bill. For exceptional service, 20% or more is common. In other countries, the standard varies significantly—some include service charges in the bill, while others have no tipping culture at all.

How much should I tip for delivery or takeout?

For food delivery, 10-15% is standard, with a minimum of $2-3 per delivery. For takeout where you pick up the food yourself, a small tip of 5-10% is appreciated but not always expected. Consider tipping more in bad weather or for large orders.

Does this tool support bill splitting?

Yes. Our tip calculator supports splitting the bill among multiple people. Simply set the number of people in your party, and the calculator will show how much each person should pay for both the bill and the tip.

What does the round-up feature do?

The round-up feature rounds the total amount up to the nearest whole number, making it easier to pay with cash. For example, if your bill is $128.50 with a 20% tip totaling $154.20, the round-up feature would suggest $155 as a clean, round total.

Are my calculations saved?

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser. All calculations are performed locally and no data is sent to any server, ensuring your privacy and security.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes. The tool is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers. Calculate tips anytime, anywhere.
