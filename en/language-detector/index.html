FAQ

Q: What languages are supported?

Supports 80+ languages including Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Thai, Vietnamese, Turkish, Dutch, Polish, Greek, Hebrew, Ukrainian and more. Near 100% accuracy for languages with different writing systems.

Q: How are mixed-language texts handled?

The tool detects each language and its percentage. Results show the primary language (highest percentage) along with all detected languages and their ratios. Great for multilingual social media content, translated documents, and code comments.

Q: Why can't some similar languages be distinguished?

Languages sharing the same writing system (e.g., English vs French, Spanish vs Portuguese) rely on common word matching for disambiguation. Short or generic text may cause misclassification. Provide at least 50 characters for best results.

Q: Can it detect programming languages?

No. This tool is designed for human natural language detection. Programming language detection (Python/JavaScript etc.) requires syntax analysis and is outside the scope of this tool.

Q: Is my data secure?

100% secure. All detection happens locally in your browser — your text never leaves your device. Safe for sensitive documents and private content.
