❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF Splitter free to use?

Yes, PDF Splitter is completely free to use. No registration or login required — all features are available immediately.

Can I use PDF Splitter on my phone?

Yes. PDF Splitter is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The interface adapts automatically to your screen size.

Are there any usage limits for PDF Splitter?

PDF Splitter has no usage limits — it's free and unlimited. Since processing is done client-side, speed depends on your device. For very large files, we recommend processing in batches for the best experience.

Do I need to install any software?

No. PDF Splitter is a web-based tool — just open it in your browser and start using it. No downloads, plugins, or extensions required.

Does PDF Splitter upload my data to a server?

No. PDF Splitter uses client-side processing — everything runs in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, and the tool works even without an internet connection.

Will the PDF quality decrease after processing?

We preserve original quality whenever possible. For compression, you can choose the compression level to balance file size and quality.
