Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will these fonts display on Instagram?

Yes! These use Unicode special characters that Instagram supports across bio, posts, stories, and comments. If a style shows as boxes on some devices, it means that device lacks the font for those characters.

Q: Why don't all styles work with Chinese?

These Unicode special characters are mainly defined for Latin alphabet (A-Z). Chinese characters are in a different Unicode range (CJK) and don't have corresponding decorative variants. For best results, use English text.

Q: How do I copy and paste?

Click the "Copy" button under any font style to copy it to your clipboard. Then open Instagram and paste it in your bio, post description, or comment.

Q: Are these fonts supported on iOS and Android?

Most modern devices support them well. Some special fonts may display differently on older iOS or specific Android versions. Test on your primary device before publishing.

Q: Does it work offline?

Yes. After loading, all conversions work offline since everything runs in your browser.
