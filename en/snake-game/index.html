About Snake Game

Free online classic Snake game. Control the snake with arrow keys to eat food and grow. Adjustable speed levels (Easy/Normal/Hard), real-time score and high score tracking. Pure Canvas rendering, no plugins needed. Perfect for casual gaming and nostalgia.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to control the snake direction
  • Eat red food to grow and earn 10 points
  • Hitting walls or yourself ends the game
  • Faster speed = bigger challenge
