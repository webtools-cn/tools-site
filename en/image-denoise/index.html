FAQ

Does denoising affect image quality?

Denoising smooths the image, which reduces noise but may slightly soften fine details. Use lower strength values to balance noise reduction and detail preservation.

What's the difference between Gaussian and Median?

Gaussian Blur applies a weighted average across the image, ideal for sensor noise. Median Filter replaces each pixel with the median of its neighbors, excellent for salt-and-pepper noise (random black/white pixels).

Does it support large images?

Yes, but very large images (5000px+) may process slower since everything runs in your browser.

What format is the output?

The denoised image is saved as PNG with lossless compression and transparency support.

Is my image data secure?

100% secure. All processing is done locally in your browser. Images are never uploaded to any server.
