❓ FAQ

What metrics does the text statistics tool measure?

This tool provides real-time stats: total characters, word count (space-separated), line count, paragraph count, sentence count, CJK character count, unique word count, average word length, estimated reading time (based on 200 wpm), and space count. All statistics update instantly as you type.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is based on the average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute. Formula: reading time (minutes) = total English words / 200. For very short text (< 200 words), it estimates by character count: reading time (seconds) = total chars / 5. Note this is a rough estimate — actual speeds vary (children ~100-150 wpm, adults ~200-300 wpm, speed readers ~400-700 wpm).

What is word frequency analysis useful for?

Word frequency analysis has many practical uses: ① SEO content optimization — checking keyword density ② Writing style analysis — identifying overused words and vocabulary diversity ③ Academic research — text and corpus analysis ④ Plagiarism detection — finding repetitive patterns ⑤ Instructional design — assessing text difficulty ⑥ Translation — understanding key vocabulary in source text.

Is my text saved to a server?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your text never reaches any server. From typing to results, all processing happens on your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Which languages are supported?

This tool supports mixed Chinese-English text: English words counted by space separation, CJK characters counted by Unicode range, sentences split by English/Chinese punctuation (. ! ? 。！？). Total character count works for all languages.
