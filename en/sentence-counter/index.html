Frequently Asked Questions

What metrics does the sentence counter measure?

This tool counts characters (with/without spaces), words, sentences, and paragraphs in real-time. It also provides estimated reading time, speaking time, Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level, and longest sentence detection.

How is readability grade calculated?

Readability is calculated using the Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level formula: 0.39×(words/sentences) + 11.8×(syllables/words) - 15.59. The result indicates the US education grade level needed to understand the text. Grade 6 = a 6th grader can comprehend it.

How accurate is the reading time estimate?

Reading time is estimated at 200-250 words per minute (standard for non-fiction English). Chinese text uses 300-400 characters per minute. Actual speed varies by reader and content difficulty. Speaking time estimates at 130-160 words per minute.
