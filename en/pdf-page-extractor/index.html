❓ Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do I need to upload my PDF?
No. Everything runs locally using PDF.js and pdf-lib. Your PDF never leaves your device. Works even offline.
❓ What page selections are supported?
Single pages (e.g., page 3), ranges (e.g., pages 1-5), or combinations (e.g., 1,3,5-8). The output PDF preserves original content and formatting.
❓ Does the extracted PDF lose quality?
No. The tool copies pages directly from the source PDF without re-rendering or compression. Fonts, images, and vector graphics are fully preserved.
❓ What's the maximum PDF size?
We recommend PDFs under 30MB. Very large files may take longer to parse in the browser.
❓ Can I reorder extracted pages?
Currently pages maintain their original order. For custom ordering, extract first then reorder using a PDF reader.
