FAQ

How to use story ideas?

Click 'Generate Idea' and the system combines a protagonist, setting, conflict, plot twist, and key item into a story hook. Use it for short stories, RPG backgrounds, or script ideas.

Can I filter by genre and tone?

Yes! Select from Fantasy/Sci-Fi/Mystery/Romance/Horror/Adventure genres and Funny/Dark/Warm/Epic/Suspenseful tones. The system prioritizes matching elements.

Can I use generated ideas commercially?

Absolutely. All story ideas are algorithmically generated with no copyright restrictions. Feel free to use them in your creative works.

Where is data stored?

Everything is processed locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server. Refresh for fresh ideas every time.
