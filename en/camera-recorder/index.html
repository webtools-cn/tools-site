About Webcam Recorder

The Webcam Recorder is a free online video recording tool that lets you capture video directly from your device's camera using just your browser. No software installation or plugins required — simply open the page and start recording. After recording, the video file downloads directly to your device without ever being uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy protection.

This tool supports multi-camera switching (front/back), resolution selection (480p/720p/1080p), and mirror flip functionality to meet various recording needs. Whether you're recording video messages, creating tutorial demonstrations, or simply capturing a quick selfie video, it's all easily accomplished.

Key Features

Multi-Camera Support: Automatically detects all cameras on your device, supporting front and back camera switching, compatible with laptops, phones, and tablets.

Resolution Selection: Choose from 480p, 720p, or 1080p resolution to balance clarity and file size according to your needs.

Live Preview: Preview the camera feed in real-time before recording to ensure framing and lighting are satisfactory.

Recording Timer: Real-time display of recording duration for easy video length control.

One-Click Download: Download video files with one click after recording. Supports WebM format compatible with all major players.

How to Use

1. Click the "Start Camera" button. The browser will request camera permission — click "Allow".

2. Select your camera device and resolution in the settings area. Confirm the preview looks good.

3. Click "Start Recording". The recording indicator will light up and the timer will start counting.

4. Click "Stop Recording" when finished. The video will automatically appear in the recordings list.

5. Click the download button on the recording item to save the video to your device.

Technical Details

This tool is built on the browser's MediaRecorder API and getUserMedia API. MediaRecorder API is a W3C standard that supports direct audio/video stream recording in the browser. Video encoding uses VP8/VP9 codecs with WebM container format output. All data processing occurs locally in the browser without any backend service dependency, ensuring user privacy and security. Modern browsers including Chrome, Edge, and Firefox all support this functionality.

FAQ

What is the Webcam Recorder tool?

The Webcam Recorder is an online tool that lets you record video directly from your browser's camera. After recording, you can download the video file. All video data is processed locally in your browser and never uploaded to any server.

Will my recorded video be uploaded to a server?

Absolutely not. This tool runs entirely in your browser. Video data only exists in your browser's memory and is never sent to any server. After recording, the video downloads directly to your computer.

What video formats are supported?

WebM format is supported by default (Chrome/Edge/Firefox). Some browsers may also support MP4 format. The tool automatically detects the best format supported by your browser.

Is there a recording time limit?

There is no time limit. You can record for as long as your browser memory allows. We recommend keeping individual recordings under 30 minutes for best performance.

How do I switch between cameras?

If your device has multiple cameras (e.g., front and back), the tool will automatically detect and list them. You can switch between cameras using the dropdown menu.

Can I adjust the video resolution?

Yes. The tool offers resolution options including 480p, 720p, and 1080p. Higher resolutions produce clearer video but larger file sizes.
