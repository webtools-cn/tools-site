How to Use the Online To

Converting with the Online To takes just three steps:

  1. Enter the Value to Convert
    Type or paste the value you want to convert into the input area. The online to supports various input formats — just enter your data and the tool will recognize it automatically.
  2. Select Source and Target Formats
    Choose the source format and the target format from the available options. The tool supports bidirectional conversion where applicable. Your settings are applied instantly.
  3. Get Your Conversion Result
    The converted result appears in real time. Click "Copy" to copy it to your clipboard, or "Download" to save it. All conversion is done locally on your device with no server upload.

FAQ

What is a To-Do List used for?

A to-do list is a fundamental task management tool that helps you record daily tasks, prioritize them, track progress, and avoid forgetting important items. It works for work planning, study tasks, personal errands, and more.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your data persists across page refreshes, but clearing browser cache will erase it.

Tips for using a to-do list effectively?

1) List tasks each morning; 2) Prioritize by importance/urgency; 3) Break big tasks into smaller steps; 4) Check off completed items for satisfaction; 5) Review and clear completed tasks regularly.

Does it support categories and sorting?

Yes. Tasks can be categorized (work, personal, study, health, etc.) and prioritized (high/medium/low). Uncompleted tasks appear first, sorted by priority.
