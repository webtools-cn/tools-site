About Net Worth Calculator

Free online net worth calculator to help you understand your financial health instantly. Net worth = Total Assets - Total Liabilities, the key metric for financial well-being.

What is net worth? Your net worth is the difference between what you own (assets) and what you owe (liabilities). A growing net worth means improving financial health.

Asset categories: Cash & savings, home (market value), investments, vehicles, retirement accounts, and other assets.

Liability categories: Mortgage balance, car loans, credit card debt, student loans, and other debts.
