💡 About This Tool

This grammar checker uses rule-based detection to find common English grammar errors, spelling mistakes, and style issues. It checks for subject-verb agreement, article usage, punctuation, comma splices, double negatives, and more.

All processing happens locally in your browser — no data is sent to any server. While it can't catch every error like a professional AI tool, it's great for quick proofreading and catching common mistakes.

Note: This is a rule-based checker and may not catch all errors. For important documents, please use a professional proofreading service.

How to Use the Grammar Checker

Validate your content with the Grammar Checker in three steps:

  1. Enter Content to Validate
    Type, paste, or upload the content you want to validate into the input area. The tool accepts direct text input and file upload for batch processing.
  2. Run Validation
    Click the "Validate" or "Check" button to analyze your content. The tool performs a thorough check and provides detailed feedback, including error locations and suggested fixes.
  3. Review Results
    View the validation results, including any errors, warnings, or success messages. If errors are found, the tool highlights their exact location to help you fix them quickly.

❓ FAQ

What types of errors does it detect?

It detects subject-verb agreement, article (a/an/the) misuse, comma splices, double negatives, commonly confused words (their/there/they're, your/you're), passive voice overuse, spelling errors, and more.

Is it as accurate as Grammarly?

No — this is a rule-based checker, not an AI-powered one. It's great for basic proofreading but won't catch subtle contextual errors. For important writing, consider professional tools.

Is my text private?

Yes. Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
