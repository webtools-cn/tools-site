📖 How to Use

Step 1: Start the Test

Focus on the text area or click the passage area, then start typing the displayed text character by character.

Step 2: Type as Fast as You Can

The timer starts automatically when you begin typing. The current character is highlighted. Correct characters turn green, wrong ones turn red.

Step 3: View Results

Once you complete typing all characters, the test ends automatically and displays your WPM, accuracy, and time statistics.

🎯 Use Cases

  • Job Preparation: Many positions require a minimum typing speed. Regular testing helps you meet requirements.
  • Daily Practice: Programmers, writers, and customer service professionals can maintain and improve their typing skills.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your typing speed improvement over time.
  • Fun Challenges: Compete with friends to make practice more engaging.

❓ FAQ

How is WPM calculated?

WPM = (Total characters typed / 5) / Time in minutes. This is the standard formula for English typing tests.

Is my data uploaded?

No. All calculations happen locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

How can I improve my typing speed?

Practice regularly, use proper finger positioning, maintain good posture, and focus on accuracy before speed.
