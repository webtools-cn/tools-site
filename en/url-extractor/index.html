How to Use URL & Email Extractor

  1. Paste text: Copy text containing URLs, emails, or phone numbers into the input area.
  2. Select types: Check the types of content to extract (URLs, emails, phones).
  3. View results: Click Extract to scan. Use tabs to view different categories.

Use cases: Extract links from scraped web data, collect contact information, SEO backlink analysis, data cleaning during migration.

FAQ

What types of content can this tool extract?
This tool extracts URLs (http/https/ftp and www links), email addresses, and phone numbers. You can choose which types to extract via checkboxes.
How do I export the extracted data?
Extracted results can be copied to clipboard with one click or downloaded as a TXT file. Each item is on a separate line, ready to import into Excel or other tools.
Is this tool secure? Does it upload my data?
Absolutely secure. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server.
Can I extract links from HTML pages?
Yes. The tool extracts all URL patterns from plain text. Paste HTML source directly into the text area, or use our HTML to Text converter first for better results.
What phone number formats are supported?
Common international phone number formats are supported, including country codes, area codes in parentheses, and numbers with hyphens or spaces.
