📖 How to Use the Online Piano

  1. Select a tone — Choose from Piano, Electric Piano, Organ, Synth, or Vibraphone

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the online piano free?

Yes, the online piano is completely free to use with no registration or login required. All features are available at no cost.

How do I play the online piano with my keyboard?

White keys map to A S D F G H J K L etc. on your keyboard. Black keys map to W E T Y U etc. Check the keyboard mapping hint displayed below the piano keys.

What devices does the online piano support?

All main devices are supported: computer keyboard for playing, touchscreen (phone and tablet) for direct tapping, and mouse click. It works smoothly on any device.

Why are some notes silent?

Please check: 1) Is your browser muted? 2) Is the volume slider at the lowest setting? 3) Some browsers require a page interaction before enabling audio. Try refreshing the page if there's no sound.

Does the online piano sound like a real piano?

The online piano uses Web Audio API synthesis to produce near-piano sounds. While it can't match a real acoustic or high-end digital piano, it offers 5 different tone presets for a varied musical experience.
