📖 FAQ

What is an audio loop?

Audio looping is a technique of repeating an audio clip seamlessly. A good loop has no audible click or gap at the connection point. Commonly used for background music, beat loops, and ambient sounds.

How to create a seamless loop?

The key is smooth waveform transition at the loop point. This tool provides two methods: 1) Crossfade - fade out the end while fading in the start for smooth transition; 2) Zero-crossing detection - automatically find zero-crossing points to avoid clicks.

Is there a limit on loop count?

Loop count is 1-50. More loops = larger output file. For long background music, use a player loop feature instead of generating a huge file.

Is audio uploaded to a server?

No. All audio processing is done locally in your browser. Audio data never leaves your device.

What scenarios is this for?

Audio loop making is ideal for: 1) Game background music; 2) Video BGM loops; 3) Beat and drum loops; 4) Meditation and ambient sounds; 5) Music sampling; 6) Presentation background audio.
