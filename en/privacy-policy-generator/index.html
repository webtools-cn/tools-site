FAQ

        
Is the generated policy legally binding?
The generated policy is a template based on common legal requirements and best practices. It covers major regulations like GDPR and CCPA but is NOT a substitute for professional legal advice. We recommend having a lawyer review it, especially if you handle sensitive data or operate in regulated industries.

    
Which regulations are supported?
GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (California), COPPA (Children's privacy), PIPL (China), LGPD (Brazil), and general privacy best practices. Select your applicable regulations during generation.

    
Can I customize the company info?
Yes. Fill in your company name, website URL, contact email, and other details before generating. The tool automatically inserts them into the correct sections.

    
How do I export the policy?
Copy the full text to clipboard with one click, or download as a TXT file. The generated content uses clean HTML formatting suitable for web use.

    
Does the policy need updates?
Yes. Privacy laws evolve continuously. Review your policy at least annually, or whenever your data processing practices change. The tool templates are updated to reflect legal changes.
