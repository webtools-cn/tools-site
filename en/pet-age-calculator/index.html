FAQ

How old is a 1-year-old dog in human years?

A 1-year-old dog is approximately 15 human years old. Different breeds age differently — small dogs mature faster, while large dogs age faster overall.

How to calculate cat age?

A 1-year-old cat ≈ 15 human years, 2 years ≈ 24, then about 4 human years per additional cat year. Cats are fully mature by age 2.

Why do large dogs age faster?

Large dogs have faster growth rates, more cell divisions, and higher oxidative stress. A Great Dane is senior at 7 while a Chihuahua can live past 15.

Is this calculation accurate?

This calculator uses veterinary research-based formulas. Individual breeds may vary. Consult your vet for breed-specific assessment.
