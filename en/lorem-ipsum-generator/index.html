Lorem Ipsum Generator is a free online tool for designers and developers to quickly generate placeholder text. Supports three modes: paragraphs, sentences, or words. Choose between plain text or HTML format output. Perfect for web design mockups, print layouts, and content previews. Everything runs locally in your browser — no registration, no uploads.

  • One-click copy to clipboard

FAQ

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used in the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It's used to demonstrate page layout before final content is ready.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All text generation happens locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

How do I copy the generated text?

Click the 'Copy' button to copy the generated text to your clipboard instantly. It works with both plain text and HTML formats.

What's the difference between modes?

'Paragraphs' generates full paragraphs (~50-100 words each). 'Sentences' generates individual sentences. 'Words' generates a list of individual Lorem Ipsum words.

Can I use the HTML output directly?

Yes. When you select HTML output format, the text is wrapped in <p> tags, ready to paste directly into your HTML code.
