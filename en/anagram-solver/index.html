How to Use the Anagram Solver

Using the Anagram Solver to unscramble letters is easy in three steps:

  1. Enter Letters
    Type the letters you want to unscramble in the input field. Spaces, punctuation, and numbers are automatically ignored. Supports 2 to 15 letters.
  2. Set Filters (Optional)
    Adjust the minimum and maximum word length to narrow down results. For Scrabble, you might set minimum to 2. For Wordle, try specific lengths.
  3. View Results
    Click the Solve button to find all matching words. Results are grouped by word length. Click any word to copy it to your clipboard instantly.

FAQ

How many letters can I enter at once?

The Anagram Solver supports up to 15 letters at a time. This covers most Scrabble, Wordle, and word puzzle scenarios. For longer queries, try splitting your letters into smaller groups.

What words are in the dictionary?

The dictionary includes over 10,000 English words, covering common vocabulary, Scrabble-legal words, and standard English terms. It's based on public English word lists and is optimized for word games.

Why can't I find a specific word?

Rare words, proper nouns (brand names, place names), and newly coined terms may not be in the dictionary. Try different letter combinations or check your spelling.

How are results sorted?

Results are grouped by word length (shortest first) and sorted alphabetically within each group. Use the min/max length filters to focus on words of a specific size.

What word games is this tool useful for?

This Anagram Solver works great for Scrabble, Wordle, Words With Friends, crossword puzzles, Boggle, and any other word game. It's also a helpful tool for vocabulary building and language learning.

Does it work offline?

Yes. All code and the word dictionary are loaded on the first page visit. After that, the tool works fully offline — no internet connection needed. Your data never leaves your device.
