How to Use

  1. Upload an image by clicking the upload area
  2. Choose a frame style: Solid, Double, Shadow, Polaroid, Rounded, or Neon
  3. Adjust thickness, color, and corner radius
  4. Preview the result in real-time
  5. Download the framed image as PNG

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?

Yes, completely free with no registration, login, or usage limits. Use it for personal and commercial projects.

Does it upload my images?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images never leave your device.

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF images are supported. The output is always a high-quality PNG file.

Can I add custom text to the frame?

Not yet — this tool focuses on decorative borders and frames. For adding text to images, try our Text on Image tool.
