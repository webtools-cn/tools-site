FAQ

        
What is the difference between ToS and Privacy Policy?
Terms of Service define the rules users must follow when using your service, including user obligations, intellectual property, payment terms, and disclaimers. Privacy Policy explains how you collect and handles personal data. Both are typically required for any online service.

    
Is the generated ToS legally binding?
The generated document is a template based on common industry standards and legal requirements. It provides a solid foundation but is NOT a substitute for professional legal advice. Have a lawyer review it, especially if your business operates in regulated industries.

    
Can I include payment/subscription terms?
Yes. Check 'Include payment terms' before generating, and the tool will add sections covering subscription fees, auto-renewal, refund policies, and cancellation terms.

    
Does it support multiple languages?
The tool supports generating terms in English and Chinese. For multilingual services, generate each version separately and ensure consistency between translations.

    
How do I add it to my website?
Copy the generated text with one click and paste it into your Terms of Service page. Or download as TXT file. Add a link to your terms in your website footer.
