📖 How to Use

What is a Drum Beat Maker?

A drum beat maker (Drum Machine) is a music creation tool that lets you arrange drum patterns by clicking on a grid. The 16-step sequencer divides one measure into 16 equal parts, each row is a drum sound, click cells to activate hits.

Available Sounds

  • Kick - Low frequency punch, the foundation
  • Snare - Mid-frequency crack, typically on beats 2 & 4
  • Hi-Hat Closed - Short metallic click, keeps the groove
  • Hi-Hat Open - Sustained metallic ring, adds variation

Use Cases

  • Quick drum pattern creation for music production
  • Drum practice and rhythm training
  • Video background music, podcast rhythm
  • Music education - demonstrate different rhythm patterns

📖 FAQ

What is a drum beat maker?

A drum beat maker (Drum Machine / Beat Maker) is an online music tool that lets you arrange drum patterns by clicking on a grid. This tool provides a 16-step sequencer with kick, snare, closed hi-hat, and open hi-hat sounds, adjustable BPM, and WAV export.

Do I need to install any software?

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API to synthesize drum sounds. No software or plugins needed. Works in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and other modern browsers.

How does the 16-step sequencer work?

The 16-step sequencer divides one measure into 16 equal steps. Each row represents a drum sound (kick, snare, etc.), and each column represents a time step. Click a cell to activate that drum hit, click again to deactivate. Press play to hear your pattern.

Can I export my beat?

Yes. Click the export button to save your drum pattern as a WAV audio file. The export is done entirely in your browser - no audio data is uploaded to any server.

What drum sounds are available?

Four core drum sounds: Kick - low frequency punch; Snare - mid-frequency crack; Hi-Hat Closed - short metallic click; Hi-Hat Open - sustained metallic ring. All sounds are synthesized via Web Audio API, no audio files loaded.
