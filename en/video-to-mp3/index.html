❓ FAQ

What video formats are supported?▼
All browser-playable formats: MP4, WebM, OGG, AVI, MOV, FLV. If your browser plays it, we can extract the audio. Output is lossless WAV format.
Will I lose audio quality?▼
No. WAV is a lossless format that preserves the original audio quality perfectly. The extracted audio sounds exactly as it does in the video, but WAV files are larger than compressed formats like MP3.
Is my data uploaded to a server?▼
No. Everything runs in your browser locally. Your video file never leaves your computer. No privacy concerns whatsoever.
How long does extraction take?▼
About 30-60 seconds for a 10-minute video on a modern computer. Keep the browser tab active during processing for best performance.
How do I get my audio file?▼
The WAV file downloads automatically when extraction completes. You can also click the download button to save it again. WAV files play in any media player.
