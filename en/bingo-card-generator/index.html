What is a Bingo Card Generator?

A bingo card generator is a pure frontend online tool that quickly creates custom 5x5 bingo game cards. It supports custom words or standard number mode, batch generation of randomly arranged cards, and is perfect for party games, classroom activities, and team building.

Key Features

Custom Words: Enter any words (one per line) and the tool randomly assigns them to bingo card cells, creating themed bingo cards.

Standard Number Mode: Use 1-75 standard bingo numbers with B:1-15, I:16-30, N:31-45, G:46-60, O:61-75.

Batch Generation: Generate 1-6 different cards at once, each with a unique random arrangement for multiplayer games.

Interactive Marking: Click cells to mark called items, making it easy to track progress during gameplay.

Print Export: Built-in print function with optimized print styles for paper bingo cards.

Tutorial

1. Enter Words: Type custom words in the text box (one per line), at least 24 words needed. Or check "Use number mode" for standard bingo numbers.

2. Configure: Select the number of cards, whether to show BINGO header, and whether the center space is FREE.

3. Generate: Click "Generate Bingo Cards" to create cards with random arrangements.

4. Play: Click cells to mark called items, or print cards for pen marking. Complete any row, column, or diagonal of 5 to win.

Background

History: Bingo originated from the Italian lottery game "Lo Giuoco del Lotto D'Italia" in the 16th century. It became popular at American carnivals in 1929 as "Beano", and was renamed "Bingo" when someone accidentally shouted it instead of "Beano".

Winning Odds: In standard 75-ball bingo, the probability of winning with a single line is about 0.04% on the 5th call, rising to 50% by the 41st call. A blackout (filling all spaces) averages about 72 calls.

Variations: Beyond standard rows, columns, and diagonals, there are four-corner bingo, X-pattern bingo, T-pattern bingo, L-pattern bingo, and blackout bingo (fill all spaces).

FAQ

What is a bingo card generator?

An online tool that quickly creates custom 5x5 bingo game cards. Input your own words and the tool randomly arranges them into unique cards, supporting batch generation and printing.

What is the standard bingo card format?

A 5x5 grid with B-I-N-G-O across the top. Each column has 5 numbers (B:1-15, I:16-30, N:31-45, G:46-60, O:61-75), with center as FREE.

Can I use custom words?

Yes. Enter custom words (one per line) and the tool randomly assigns them to cells. Perfect for themed bingo games.

Is my data uploaded to a server?

No. This tool runs entirely in your browser. All generation is performed locally with no server uploads.

How do you win at bingo?

Common winning patterns: any complete row, column, or diagonal of 5 marked cells. Some variations require all four corners or a complete blackout (all 25 cells).

Can I generate multiple different cards?

Yes. Select 1-6 cards. Each has a unique random arrangement, perfect for multiplayer games.
