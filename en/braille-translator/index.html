💡 About Braille

Braille is a tactile writing system used by people who are visually impaired. It was invented by Louis Braille in 1824.

Each Braille character is made of a 2x3 dot matrix (6 dots), giving 64 possible combinations.

This translator supports Grade 1 Braille (letter-by-letter) for English and basic Chinese characters.
