How to Use

Enter the coordinates of two points, select 2D or 3D mode, and click calculate.

Uses the Euclidean distance formula: d = √[(x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)² + (z₂-z₁)²]

All calculations happen in your browser — no data is uploaded to any server.
