FAQ

What are the benefits of converting GIF to video?

Video formats (MP4/WEBM) are much more compressed than GIF, typically reducing file size by 80-90%. Videos also offer better color, precise playback control, and the ability to add audio.

Is my GIF file safe?

Absolutely! All processing is done entirely in your browser. Your GIF is never uploaded to any server. You can even use this tool offline.

What GIF size is supported?

We recommend GIF files under 50MB. Larger files may take longer to process. If your browser becomes unresponsive, try using WEBM format which is faster to encode.

WEBM vs MP4 - which should I choose?

WEBM encodes faster in the browser and supports transparency. MP4 has wider device compatibility but encodes slower and doesn't support transparent backgrounds.
