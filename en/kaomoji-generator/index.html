FAQ

Q1: What is kaomoji?

Kaomoji are Japanese-style text emoticons made from Unicode characters. Unlike Western emoticons (like :-)), kaomoji are upright and don't require tilting your head. For example, (≧▽≦) means happy and (╥﹏╥) means sad.

Q2: How do I use kaomoji on my phone?

Click any kaomoji to copy it to your clipboard, then paste it into any app like WhatsApp, Twitter, or Discord. Some complex kaomoji may not display correctly in all apps.

Q3: What's the difference between kaomoji and emoji?

Kaomoji are text-based expressions using Unicode characters (like ᕙ(⇀‸↼‶)ᕗ), while emoji are pictographs rendered by the OS (like 😀). Kaomoji are more flexible and can create infinite combinations.
