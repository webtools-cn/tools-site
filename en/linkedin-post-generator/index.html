Frequently Asked Questions

What are LinkedIn post best practices?

Best LinkedIn posts are 150-200 words. Use short paragraphs (1-2 sentences), add emojis for readability, end with a question to encourage engagement, and include 3-5 relevant hashtags. Posts with images get 3x more engagement.

When is the best time to post on LinkedIn?

Best times are Tuesday through Thursday during work hours (8-10 AM or 12-1 PM). Avoid weekends and holidays. Use LinkedIn Analytics to find your specific audience's most active times.

Can I use generated content commercially?
