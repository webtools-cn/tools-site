📖 MAC Address Format Guide

• Colon-separated: AA:BB:CC:DD:EE:FF (most common)

• Dash-separated: AA-BB-CC-DD-EE-FF (Windows style)

• Dot-separated: AABB.CCDD.EEFF (Cisco devices)

• No separator: AABBCCDDEEFF (some APIs)

• OUI prefix: First 3 bytes identify the vendor
