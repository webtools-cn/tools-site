About Tic Tac Toe

Free online Tic Tac Toe game. Play against AI (3 difficulty levels: Easy/Medium/Hard) or 2-player mode. Click to place X or O on the 3×3 grid. Real-time win/draw detection and game history. Client-side only, no registration. Perfect for family fun and brain training.

Rules

  • 3×3 grid. Player X goes first, then O
  • vs AI mode: AI adapts to difficulty level
  • Get three in a row (horizontal, vertical, diagonal) to win
  • If board fills with no winner, it's a draw
