How to Use

Enter the coordinates of two endpoints, select 2D or 3D mode, and click calculate.

Midpoint formula: M = ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2, (z₁+z₂)/2)

All calculations happen in your browser — no data is uploaded to any server.
